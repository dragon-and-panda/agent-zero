# Marketplace Partner Profiles

Quick reference for agents when tailoring listings or negotiating with platform contacts.

## Craigslist (CL)
- **Audience:** Local bargain hunters, hobbyists, DIY community.
- **Strengths:** High reach, zero commission, flexible categories.
- **Risks:** Higher fraud attempts; listings can be ghosted if reposted too often.
- **Best Use:** Bulky goods, vehicles, furniture, appliances where local pickup is logical.
- **Operational Tips:** Rotate subject lines to avoid spam filters; monitor replies via relay email to prevent exposing seller contact info.

## Mercari
- **Audience:** Mobile-first shoppers, collectors, fashion/tech enthusiasts.
- **Strengths:** Built-in payment + shipping, reputation system, buyer protection.
- **Fees:** 10% marketplace fee + payment processing (2.9% + $0.50). Include this in Autonomy Comptroller budget reports.
- **Best Use:** Consumer electronics, fashion, collectibles that benefit from national reach.
- **Operational Tips:** Ship within 24 hours to maintain seller rating; enable smart pricing rules to auto-adjust within allowed floor/ceiling.

## Nextdoor
- **Audience:** Verified neighbors within a few-mile radius.
- **Strengths:** Trust via neighborhood identity, built-in messaging, local discovery feed.
- **Risks:** Community guidelines strictly prohibit commercial spam; repeated bumps may be throttled.
