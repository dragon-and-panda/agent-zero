# Strategy Intake Queue

Use this queue to capture candidate ventures before they enter execution. Each item should be scored with `instruments/strategy/score.sh` and tagged `GO`, `HOLD`, or `REJECT`.

| Opportunity | Summary | Status | Reason |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Inbox-to-CRM Hygiene Service | Organize a consenting owner's own email corpus into contact graph, CRM tags, and follow-up actions | GO | High compliance, fast value, recurring service potential |
| Public-Web Research Briefs | Build competitor/prospect/supplier briefs from public or client-provided sources | GO | Fast to deliver, low compliance risk, easy to productize |
| Autonomous Listing Concierge | Use the listing-service stack to generate and manage marketplace listings | GO | Existing blueprint in repo and multiple monetization angles |
| Course + Character Content Funnel | Turn documented workflows into tutorials and story-based content | HOLD | Strong upside, but slower monetization and needs a content cadence |
| Leveraged Forex trading | Deploy capital into trading strategies after revenue is earned | HOLD | Requires separate risk framework and should not be an early dependency |
| Personal email list brokerage | Sell harvested or compiled addresses from inboxes or files | REJECT | Prohibited by compliance pack; privacy, spam, and platform risk |
