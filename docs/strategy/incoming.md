## Intake Questions

Before moving an idea into execution:

1. Who pays for this and why now?
2. What data rights are required?
3. What is the smallest deliverable that proves value?
4. Can the workflow survive if one platform disappears?
5. What is the rollback path if quality or compliance fails?
