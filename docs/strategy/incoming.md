# Strategy Intake Triage

Use this page to convert raw mission ideas into approved execution lanes.

## Triage outcomes

### GO
Ideas that are legal, consent-based, operationally realistic, and aligned with the current cash-flow strategy.

Examples:
- Consent-based inbox-to-CRM cleanup for a client using their own Google Workspace export or connected mailbox.
- Lead magnet funnels where users explicitly opt in to receive resources or offers.
- Productized research briefs sold as subscriptions or one-off reports.
- Marketplace listing optimization services for client-owned assets.

### HOLD
Ideas that may be legal but need more validation, controls, or infrastructure before activation.

Examples:
- AI-assisted outbound systems that require proof of consent records and unsubscribe handling.
- Affiliate sites that need durable traffic acquisition before monetization.
- Trading or betting systems prior to passing a simulation and reserve-capital gate.

### REJECT
Ideas that rely on non-consensual data extraction, privacy violations, spam, or platform abuse.

Examples:
- Building or selling scraped email lists.
- Using inbox or account data without explicit authorization from the owner.
- Impersonation, fake reviews, deceptive landing pages, or ToS-evasion tactics.
- Reselling personal data gathered from public websites, breaches, or scraped directories.
