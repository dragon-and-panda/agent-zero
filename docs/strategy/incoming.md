## Intake Fields

For each new opportunity, capture:

- name,
- target buyer,
- outcome delivered,
- acquisition path,
- delivery workflow,
- legal/compliance notes,
- capital needs,
- dependencies,
- fallback plan.

## Decision Rule

- **GO**: high compliance, fast launch, good margin, repeatable.
- **HOLD**: viable later, but blocked by timing, proof, or risk.
- **REJECT**: depends on privacy abuse, spam, deception, unauthorized data, or unjustified speculation.
