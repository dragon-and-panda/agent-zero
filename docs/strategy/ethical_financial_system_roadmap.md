# Ethical Agentic Financial System Roadmap

This roadmap translates the mission into an autonomous, legally safe execution plan.

## 1) Mission Statement

Build a self-sustaining online financial engine using an agentic framework that:
- Creates repeatable revenue,
- Reinvests capital responsibly,
- Documents all decisions,
- Converts execution into educational media assets.

## 2) Core Operating Principles

1. Compliance before growth.
2. Consent before contact.
3. Cash-flow before complexity.
4. Measure everything; keep only what compounds.

## 3) Responsibility Mapping

## 3.1 Agentic Framework
- Stand up role-based agents:
  - Apex Orchestrator
  - Revenue Operator
  - Compliance Guardian
  - Content Producer
  - Trading Analyst
- Add reusable instruments for:
  - Lead intake validation
  - Offer generation
  - Campaign analytics
  - Weekly P&L and transfer logs

## 3.2 Data Work (RAG + Email)
- Use RAG on approved mailbox data for:
  - Relationship intelligence,
  - Follow-up reminders,
  - Topic clustering and response quality.
- Build contact lists only from lawful/permissioned sources with consent metadata.
- Use Orange Data Mining (or equivalent) for segmentation, deduplication, and scoring.

## 3.3 Monetization Phase 1 (0-90 days)
- Launch 1-2 cash-flow services:
  - AI automation setup for small businesses
  - Listing/content optimization as a productized service
- Launch one audience asset:
  - Opt-in newsletter + free lead magnet
- Launch one digital product:
  - Mini-course, templates, or SOP bundle

## 3.4 Financial Expansion (Post Phase 1)
- Trading progression:
  1. Paper trade + journal
  2. Small live allocation with hard risk limits
  3. Scale only after consistent edge metrics
- Prioritize strategy validation (expectancy, drawdown, consistency) over raw win rate.

## 3.5 Financial Management
- Weekly revenue close:
  - Reconcile income, costs, and reserves
  - Transfer net owner draw to designated cash destination
  - Archive transfer evidence in finance logs

## 3.6 Content + Media
- Maintain process artifacts:
  - Mission diary updates
  - Before/after case studies
  - Tooling playbooks
- Convert artifacts into:
  - Course modules
  - YouTube narrative episodes
- Story concept:
  - Anthropomorphic guide character narrates the journey toward funding a mech suit + robot body, while teaching practical business systems.

## 4) Execution Cadence

### Daily
- Prospecting from approved channels
- Delivery on active client/product tasks
- KPI checkpoint (leads, conversion, cash collected)

### Weekly
- Revenue review + transfer reconciliation
- Experiment review (offers, channels, pricing)
- Content production sprint (one publishable asset)

### Monthly
- Portfolio rebalance across service/product/trading buckets
- Kill/keep/scale decisions based on metrics
- Strategy refresh in `docs/strategy/incoming.md`

## 5) KPI Stack

- Revenue: gross, net, recurring share
- Sales: lead->call, call->close, close cycle time
- Delivery: turnaround time, satisfaction, retention
- Audience: opt-in growth, open/click rates, conversion to offer
- Trading: max drawdown, expectancy, Sharpe-like stability proxy

## 6) First 30-Day Build Plan

1. Finalize offers and pricing.
2. Set up consent-based lead capture + CRM schema.
3. Build RAG-assisted workflow for inbox triage and follow-up drafting.
4. Ship first educational product draft.
5. Publish 4 short-form content pieces + 1 long-form tutorial.
6. Start paper-trading journal with strict review template.

## 7) Stop Conditions

Pause any activity if:
- It violates `docs/policies/compliance_pack.md`,
- Cash conversion remains negative for 2+ cycles,
- Risk metrics breach predefined thresholds.
