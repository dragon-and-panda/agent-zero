# Character Brief — Narrator for the Revenue Journey

## Working Concept

Create an anthropomorphic narrator who frames the business-building process as a long-form upgrade quest: every legal win, reusable system, and documented lesson becomes another step toward funding a mech suit and, eventually, a robotic body.

## Character Direction

- archetype: persistent tinkerer-strategist
- tone: witty, determined, transparent, slightly dramatic
- visual cues: workshop jacket, improvised tools, modular armor parts earned over time
- role: narrator, explainer, and accountability voice

## Narrative Rules

- the character celebrates systems, not shortcuts
- revenue comes from useful products and real customers
- mistakes become lessons and process upgrades
- illegal or manipulative tactics are framed as false shortcuts that damage the mission

## Content Formats

### 1. Tutorial / Course Layer

Turn each workflow into:

- a lesson objective
- a checklist
- a demo
- a retro note on what changed

### 2. YouTube Story Layer

Each episode should answer:

1. what mission was attempted
2. what system was built or improved
3. what obstacle appeared
4. what result moved the mech-suit fund forward

### 3. Social Clips

Extract short clips around:

- hard-earned insights
- failures and fixes
- before/after automation wins
- scorecard updates

## Episode Arc Template

1. mission brief
2. risk and compliance check
3. build montage
4. first customer or system result
5. lessons learned
6. upgrade unlocked

## Asset Backlog

- character sheet
- voice and catchphrase guide
- intro and outro script
- episode scoreboard template
- mech upgrade milestone chart

## Suggested Framing

The story is not "get rich fast." It is "build lawful systems that compound until the upgrades are earned."
