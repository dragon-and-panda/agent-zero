# Financial System Mission (Compliant Autonomy)

This mission translates the objective ("build a self-sustaining online financial system") into an agent-executable plan that is legal, auditable, and scalable.

---

## 1. Mission Objective

Build a recurring-income digital operation that:

1. Uses an agentic framework for research, automation, and execution.
2. Monetizes lawful online products/services first.
3. Reinvests profits into disciplined, risk-managed trading workflows.
4. Documents the entire journey as educational media content.

---

## 2. Non-Negotiable Guardrails

The system must **not** perform unlawful or abusive actions.

- Do not scrape or sell private contact data from email accounts.
- Do not use email addresses for outreach without a valid lawful basis and opt-out flow.
- Do not access inboxes without explicit account-owner authorization (OAuth).
- Do not execute live trading strategies that fail risk limits or paper-trading benchmarks.

If an opportunity conflicts with privacy, anti-spam, or platform terms, the agent rejects it and proposes alternatives.

## 3. Agentic Framework

### 3.1 Core Roles

| Role | Purpose | Primary Outputs |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Mission Orchestrator | Sets priorities, schedules runs, enforces guardrails | Weekly sprint plan, KPI board |

1. Plan weekly hypotheses.
2. Execute daily tasks via role agents.

4. Retrospective every 7 days.
5. Promote validated playbooks to persistent knowledge.

## 4. Data Pipeline (RAG + Gmail, Consent-First)

### 4.1 Approved Data Sources

- Gmail metadata/content from authenticated, authorized account(s).
- Sent/received headers (`From`, `To`, `Cc`) for relationship mapping.
- User-provided files (CSV/CRM exports) with lawful ownership.

### 4.2 Processing Pipeline

1. Ingest messages and files.
2. Extract contact candidates from headers.
3. Normalize/dedupe (`lowercase`, canonical domains, hash keys).
4. Attach `consent_status`:
   - `opt_in`
   - `contractual`
   - `legitimate_interest_review`
   - `do_not_contact`
5. Store vectors for RAG retrieval (topic clustering, relationship context, prior conversations).
6. Export structured datasets to Orange DataScaping for segmentation and analysis.

### 4.3 Mandatory Compliance Fields

- `source_system`
- `first_seen_at`
- `last_seen_at`
- `consent_status`
- `suppression_reason`
- `retention_expiry`

Any record without clear lawful basis is automatically suppressed.

## 5. Monetization - Phase 1 (Legal Channels)

Replace "sell scraped email lists" with sustainable alternatives:

1. **Opt-in Newsletter Asset**
   - Build list via lead magnets and landing pages.
   - Monetize via sponsorships, affiliate offers, and premium tiers.
2. **Research-as-a-Service**
   - Sell curated market or niche intelligence reports.
3. **Automation Services**
   - Offer agent setup, workflow automation, or content systems for clients.
4. **Digital Products**
   - Templates, SOP bundles, mini-courses, and prompt packs.

### 5.1 Weekly Growth Targets

- +10% qualified subscriber growth
- CAC payback < 45 days
- Positive contribution margin per offer

---

## 6. Financial Expansion (Post Phase 1)

Trading begins only after stable operating cashflow and process maturity.

### 6.1 Staged Rollout

1. **Paper Trading (minimum 60 days)**
   - Forex strategy validation only.
2. **Micro Capital Deployment**
   - Start with capped risk allocation.
3. **Scaled Deployment**
   - Increase only after meeting risk-adjusted thresholds.

### 6.2 Strategy Validation Metrics

- Profit factor >= 1.3
- Max drawdown <= 8%
- Sharpe (or proxy) >= 1.0
- No single strategy > 40% of deployed risk

## 7. Treasury and Cash Management

### 7.1 Cashflow Rules

- Split net income:
  - 50% operating reserve
  - 30% growth/reinvestment
  - 20% transfer payout
- Maintain monthly reconciliation log.

### 7.2 Cash App Transfer Control

Target payout destination: `$Nicsins`.

Because direct automation pathways can change, keep transfer execution behind a verified checklist:

1. Confirm available cash after reserves.
2. Confirm transfer amount and date in ledger.
3. Execute transfer.
4. Record transaction ID/screenshot hash.

## 10. KPIs and Stop Conditions

### Primary KPIs
- Monthly recurring revenue
- Net profit margin
- Qualified list growth (opt-in only)
- Content output cadence
- Paper trading risk metrics

### Stop Conditions
- Any compliance breach
- Drawdown beyond defined limit
- Two consecutive weeks of negative unit economics

On stop, freeze expansion and run incident review before resuming.
