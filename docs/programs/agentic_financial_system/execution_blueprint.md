# Agentic Financial System — Execution Blueprint (Compliance-First)

## 1) Objective
Build a self-sustaining, compounding online income system operated by an agentic framework, with strict legal/ethical controls and measurable KPIs.

---

## 2) Mission Translation Into Actionable, Safe Workstreams

### A. Agentic Framework
- Orchestrate specialized agents for:
  - Strategy and monetization.
  - Data ingestion and RAG.
  - Compliance/risk.
  - Finance operations.
  - Content production.
- Require telemetry on every cycle: `goal -> action -> result -> next hypothesis`.

### B. Data Extraction and RAG
- Allowed data sources:
  - User-authorized exports/APIs from owned inboxes and CRMs.
  - Sent/received/CC metadata where lawful and consent-compatible.
  - First-party operational files (client records, support logs, call notes).
- Mandatory controls:
  - Consent + lawful-basis tagging per contact.
  - Suppression list enforcement.
  - Dedupe + integrity checks.
  - Encryption at rest and access logging.
- Analysis tooling:
  - Use Orange DataScaping (or Orange Data Mining workflows) for clustering, segmentation, and campaign diagnostics.
  - Feed outputs into RAG for retrieval-based personalization and internal decision support.

### C. Monetization (Phase 1)
- Prohibited: selling scraped or non-consensual email lists.
- Approved alternatives:
  1. Productized outbound systems for clients (consent-based prospecting).
  2. Newsletter/media asset growth with opt-in lead magnets.
  3. B2B research/intelligence products derived from non-personal or licensed data.
  4. Managed automation services (workflow setup, CRM ops, analytics).
- Weekly growth loop:
  - Launch one offer experiment.
  - Measure conversion + delivery cost.
  - Keep/kill/iterate.

### D. Financial Expansion (Post-Phase-1)
- Precondition: stable positive cashflow from Phase 1.
- Trading sequence:
  1. Strategy research.
  2. Backtesting.
  3. Paper trading.
  4. Small-risk live deployment with hard drawdown limits.
- Prioritize risk management over return optimization.

### E. Financial Management
- Establish a payout SOP:
  - Revenue reconciliation.
  - Tax/event tagging.
  - Approved transfer destination checklist (including designated accounts).
  - Weekly finance audit report.

### F. Content Creation Engine
- Produce three synchronized artifacts:
  1. Internal operating log (what worked, failed, changed).
  2. Tutorial/course modules (repeatable system).
  3. YouTube narrative episodes.
- Narrative anchor:
  - Anthropomorphic guide character documenting the mission to fund a mech suit + robot body.
  - Blend education + story progression in each episode.

---

## 3) System Architecture (MVP)

1. **Ingestion Layer**
   - Connectors for mailbox exports/API pulls.
   - Normalization into canonical contact/events schema.

2. **Compliance Gate**
   - Rule engine to reject non-compliant contacts/actions.
   - Policy checks before any outbound step.

3. **Knowledge + RAG Layer**
   - Vector index + metadata filters (consent status, segment, recency).
   - Retrieval for strategy, personalization, and support tasks.

4. **Execution Layer**
   - Campaign/orchestration tools.
   - Offer experiment runner.
   - Reporting and dashboard generation.

5. **Capital Layer**
   - Treasury dashboard.
   - Trading sandbox and risk monitor.

6. **Media Layer**
   - Script generator.
   - Storyboard tracker.
   - Publish checklist.

---

## 4) Initial KPIs
- Weekly qualified opt-ins.
- Offer conversion rate.
- Revenue per experiment cycle.
- Fulfillment margin.
- RAG retrieval precision for monetization tasks.
- Compliance incidents (target: zero).
- Content cadence adherence (episodes/week).
