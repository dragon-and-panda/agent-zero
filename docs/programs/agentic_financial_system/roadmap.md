# Agentic Financial System — Program Roadmap

This roadmap operationalizes a self-sustaining online income system using Agent Zero while enforcing the compliance guardrails in `docs/policies/compliance_pack.md`.

---

## 1) Mission Objective
- Build a repeatable, self-improving financial engine from online ventures.
- Reinvest profits into higher-leverage channels only after risk controls are proven.
- Produce public-facing documentation and educational content from all major workflows.

## 2) Operating Constraints
- No sale of harvested email lists.
- No unauthorized email extraction from Gmail or third-party accounts.
- Use only consent-based audience growth and lawful outreach methods.
- Apply risk and compliance checks before any monetization expansion or capital deployment.

## 3) Phase Plan

### Phase A — Agentic Foundation (Weeks 1-2)
1. Stand up mission agents:
   - Framework Architect
   - Data Operations Steward
   - Revenue Operator
   - Risk & Treasury Governor
   - Content Studio Producer
2. Define telemetry:
   - Revenue/day, conversion rate, CAC, LTV proxy, automation uptime.
3. Create SOP pack for repeatable execution:
   - lead capture flow,
   - outreach cadence,
   - offer testing loop,
   - transfer logging protocol.

### Phase B — Compliant Data Engine (Weeks 2-4)
1. Implement RAG over owner-approved communications and documents for:
   - intent tagging,
   - lead qualification support,
   - response drafting.
2. Build contact graph from approved sources:
   - opt-in forms,
   - sent/received relationship metadata where permission exists,
   - CRM and sanctioned internal files.
3. Normalize and classify with Orange DataScaping:
   - deduplicate contacts,
   - segment by interest and engagement,
   - assign outreach priority scores.

Deliverable: a clean, consent-based lead intelligence dataset with suppression/unsubscribe support.

### Phase C — Monetization Phase 1 (Weeks 4-8)
1. Launch low-friction revenue channels:
   - productized services,
   - affiliate funnels,
   - digital templates/courses.
2. Run weekly experiments:
   - one offer test,
   - one pricing test,
   - one channel test.
3. Grow audience legally:
   - lead magnets,
   - content-driven opt-in funnels,
   - partner co-marketing.

Deliverable: first stable monthly revenue baseline.

### Phase D — Capital Expansion (Weeks 8+)
1. Keep business cashflow and trading capital in separate ledgers.
2. Start with paper-trading strategies (Forex and alternatives):
   - trend-following,
   - mean reversion,
   - event filter rules.
3. Promote only validated strategies to live capital under strict drawdown limits.

Deliverable: controlled, data-backed capital allocation playbook.

### Phase E — Documentation and Story IP (Continuous)
1. Maintain mission log, metrics snapshots, and retrospectives.
2. Convert workflows into:
   - written tutorial/course,
   - YouTube narrative arc,
   - reusable templates/checklists.
3. Develop an anthropomorphic narrator character:
   - identity, visual style, voice profile,
   - episode structure tracking the "mech suit and robot body" storyline.

Deliverable: educational content engine that compounds reach and leads.

---

## 4) Weekly Control Loop
1. Observe metrics and failures.
2. Diagnose funnel or process bottlenecks.
3. Design one high-impact experiment per active channel.
4. Deploy changes in small batches.
5. Reflect and record lessons in program journal.

## 5) KPI Targets
- Week 2: instrumentation complete; baseline dashboard live.
- Week 4: consent-based lead database online with active segmentation.
- Week 8: at least one offer/channel pair meeting target unit economics.
- Ongoing: positive net cashflow with full audit trail and compliance pass rate of 100%.

## 6) Risk Register (Initial)
- Data/privacy breach risk -> mitigated via consent tracking and redaction by default.
- Platform enforcement risk -> mitigated via ToS review before each channel launch.
- Trading loss risk -> mitigated via staged capital deployment and hard drawdown limits.
- Execution drift risk -> mitigated via weekly retros + backlog grooming.
