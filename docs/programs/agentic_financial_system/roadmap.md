# Agentic Financial System - Program Roadmap

> Mission: build a self-sustaining online financial engine using compliant automation, measurable execution, and repeatable content.

## 1) Non-Negotiable Guardrails

- Use only consented and authorized data sources.
- Do not sell or transfer personal email lists harvested from inboxes.
- Follow anti-spam, privacy, and data protection laws (for example: CAN-SPAM, GDPR, CCPA where applicable).
- Treat high-risk activities (capital allocation and trading) as controlled processes with strict risk limits.
- Keep an auditable operations log for every automation run.

## 2) Agentic Framework (Core Architecture)

Create an adaptable multi-agent system with these roles:

1. **Orchestrator Agent**  
   Routes tasks, tracks state, and enforces guardrails.
2. **Toolsmith Agent**  
   Generates and refines tools (extractors, parsers, report generators).
3. **Data Steward Agent**  
   Handles ingestion, normalization, and compliance checks.
4. **Monetization Agent**  
   Tests approved revenue channels and tracks unit economics.
5. **Treasury Agent**  
   Manages cashflow reporting and transfer checklists.
6. **Content Studio Agent**  
   Converts weekly operations into course and video assets.

## 3) Data Extraction and Organization

### 3.1 Gmail + RAG Pipeline

- Connect Gmail through OAuth with explicit account owner authorization.
- Index only needed fields into a RAG store:
  - sender
  - recipients (to, cc, bcc when available and permitted)
  - timestamp
  - thread metadata
  - labels and business intent tags
- Build retrieval workflows for:
  - partner discovery
  - customer support follow-up
  - lead qualification from opt-in conversations

### 3.2 Address List Policy

Maintain segmented lists for operational use only:

- inbound contacts
- outbound contacts
- copied stakeholders
- CRM/imported permission-based contacts

Each record should include:

- source
- consent status
- last contact date
- segment
- suppression flag

### 3.3 Orange DataScaping Workflow

- Run cleaning and deduplication.
- Segment by engagement, domain type, and topic clusters.
- Produce weekly dashboards: contact growth, response rate, and deliverability risk.

## 4) Monetization - Phase 1 (Compliant Channels)

Prioritize legal and repeatable online revenue streams:

1. **Permission-based newsletter sponsorships**
2. **Affiliate offers matched to audience intent**
3. **Digital products (templates, automation packs, playbooks)**
4. **Serviceized automation audits for small businesses**

Acquisition expansion methods:

- SEO content funnels
- lead magnets with explicit opt-in
- partnerships and co-marketing
- community distribution and webinars

## 5) Financial Expansion - Post Phase 1

Only after stable positive cashflow:

1. Build a paper-trading system first.
2. Define strategy hypotheses (for example trend-following, mean reversion, breakout).
3. Backtest with realistic slippage and fees.
4. Graduate to tiny live sizing with hard drawdown limits.
5. Scale only on statistically consistent performance.

Risk controls:

- max daily loss
- max weekly loss
- kill switch on breach
- mandatory trade journal and post-trade review

## 6) Treasury and Cash Operations

- Maintain an earnings ledger by channel.
- Reconcile weekly net profit after platform and tooling costs.
- Trigger a transfer checklist to deposit approved funds into Cash App account **$Nicsins**.
- Keep timestamped receipts and reconciliation notes.

## 7) Content Creation System

### 7.1 Documentation

- Keep a mission diary for every iteration.
- Capture: inputs, decisions, outputs, metrics, blockers, next actions.

### 7.2 Course and YouTube Pipeline

- Convert weekly logs into:
  - lesson outline
  - script draft
  - visual storyboard
  - publication checklist

### 7.3 Character Narrative

Develop an anthropomorphic narrator persona:

- character bible (voice, motive, constraints)
- episodic arc (earning capital for mech suit and robot body)
- tie each episode to one real operational milestone

## 8) 30-60-90 Execution Targets

### Day 0-30

- Stand up agent roles, Gmail ingestion, RAG index, and Orange segmentation.
- Ship first compliant offer funnel.
- Publish first mission episode.

### Day 31-60

- Optimize conversion and retention loops.
- Launch second and third monetization channels.
- Finalize paper-trading analytics stack.

### Day 61-90

- Reach stable monthly positive cashflow.
- Start micro-sized live trading under risk controls.
- Release public tutorial baseline and recurring YouTube cadence.

## 9) Core Program KPIs

- Monthly recurring revenue
- Lead-to-customer conversion rate
- Email engagement and deliverability
- Content output consistency
- Trading expectancy and drawdown (post Phase 1 only)
- Cash transfer completion rate and reconciliation accuracy
