# Agentic Financial System — Mission Charter (Compliant)

## 1) Mission Objective
Build a self-sustaining online financial system that compounds revenue through:
- Ethical digital ventures,
- Compliance-first automation,
- Disciplined treasury and investment operations,
- Ongoing audience-driven content.

This charter converts the requested vision into a legal and durable execution model.

---

## 2) Non-Negotiable Guardrails
1. **No sale of personal email lists** collected from inboxes, sent mail, CC fields, or scraped files.
2. **No unauthorized data extraction** from Google accounts or third-party sources.
3. **Consent and lawful basis required** before any outreach or enrichment activity.
4. **Follow platform and legal requirements** (Terms of Service, CAN-SPAM, GDPR/CCPA where applicable).
5. **Risk controls required** before capital deployment into volatile instruments.

---

## 3) Agentic Framework Responsibilities

### A. Apex Orchestrator
- Converts mission into weekly OKRs.
- Maintains risk register and stop/go criteria.
- Triggers retrospectives and backlog reprioritization.

### B. Toolsmith Agent
- Builds reusable instruments for data intake, scoring, and reporting.
- Maintains MCP/API adapters and automation scripts.

### C. Data Steward Agent
- Executes consented data ingestion and normalization pipelines.
- Applies deduplication, tagging, and contact quality scoring.
- Stores provenance metadata for auditability.

### D. Growth Monetization Agent
- Runs ethical lead magnets, partnerships, and affiliate campaigns.
- Optimizes conversion funnels and unit economics.

### E. Treasury & Trading Agent
- Maintains capital allocation policy and drawdown limits.
- Runs paper-trading validation before live deployment.
- Tracks realized/unrealized P&L and exposure limits.

### F. Content Studio Agent
- Documents process, decisions, and outcomes.
- Produces tutorial/course modules and YouTube adaptation assets.
- Maintains character/story continuity.

## 4) Data Strategy (RAG + Email Intelligence, Legal Path)

### 4.1 Allowed Sources
- User-owned mailbox exports or APIs with explicit owner authorization.
- CRM/contact records with proven opt-in or legitimate business basis.
- Public/business data sources that permit enrichment use.

### 4.2 Required Pipeline
1. **Ingest:** Pull approved data sources (Gmail API/export, CRM, CSV).
2. **Normalize:** Parse sender/recipient/CC/BCC metadata and domains.
3. **Classify:** Distinguish personal vs. business contacts and confidence.
4. **Consent Filter:** Keep only records eligible for outreach use.
5. **RAG Index:** Build retrieval corpus for segmentation and campaign drafting.
6. **Audit Log:** Persist source, timestamp, legal basis, and usage constraints.

### 4.3 Orange DataScaping Role
- Use Orange DataScaping for cluster analysis, de-duplication, and segmentation.
- Produce export profiles:
  - Partner candidates
  - Affiliate prospects
  - Newsletter sponsorship leads
  - Existing audience reactivation cohorts

## 5) Monetization — Phase 1 (Compliant)
Replace list-selling with durable, legal revenue channels:

1. **Newsletter Sponsorship Brokerage**
   - Package audience segments and campaign performance reports.
2. **Affiliate Distribution**
   - Promote vetted SaaS/education/finance tools to opt-in segments.
3. **B2B Lead-Gen Service**
   - Provide outreach operations using consented/professionally sourced data.
4. **Micro-Offer Products**
   - Sell templates, automations, prompt packs, or mini-courses.
5. **Done-For-You Automation Setup**
   - Build RAG + outreach ops stacks for clients.

### Core KPIs
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Cost per qualified lead (CPL)
- Conversion rate by segment
- Refund/chargeback rate
- Compliance incident count (target: zero)

---

## 6) Financial Expansion (Post Phase 1)

### 6.1 Capital Allocation Ladder
1. Maintain operating reserve (minimum 6 months runway).
2. Reinvest into highest-ROI ventures and distribution.
3. Allocate only risk budget to trading experiments.

### 6.2 Trading Rollout
1. Strategy research and backtest.
2. Paper trading with strict metrics.
3. Small live capital pilot with hard risk rules:
   - Max risk per trade,
   - Max daily drawdown,
   - Weekly stop-loss circuit breaker.

### 6.3 Forex Constraints
- Start only after paper strategy reaches target Sharpe/win/loss profile.
- Use a pre-trade checklist and post-trade journal for every position.

## 7) Cash Management and Payout Ops

- Define a payout cadence (e.g., weekly) and reconciliation process.
- Route net distributions to designated account (`$Nicsins`) only after reconciliation and reserve checks.

## 10) Success Definition
The mission succeeds when revenue growth is **repeatable, auditable, and compliant**, with a system that can autonomously:
- discover opportunities,
- build and improve tools,
- convert demand ethically,
- and compound capital without violating privacy or platform rules.
