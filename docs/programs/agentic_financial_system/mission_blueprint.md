# Agentic Financial System — Mission Blueprint

## Mission
Build a self-sustaining online income system that can compound into larger financial opportunities, while staying legal, ethical, and auditable.

## Non-Negotiable Guardrails
- Use only data and accounts you are authorized to access.
- Do **not** sell personal email addresses or any private contact data.
- Use contact data only for consent-based outreach and first-party operations.
- Follow platform terms and applicable regulations (CAN-SPAM, GDPR/UK GDPR, CCPA, and local law).
- Store sensitive data securely, with least-privilege access and deletion policies.

---

## Responsibility 1: Agentic Framework

### Objective
Create a flexible system that can plan, execute, evaluate, and improve workflows with minimal manual intervention.

### System Roles
- **Orchestrator Agent:** prioritizes goals, launches sub-agents, and tracks KPIs.
- **Toolsmith Agent:** creates/revises scripts and adapters for data, analytics, and publishing.
- **Compliance Agent:** blocks unsafe/illegal actions and enforces guardrails.
- **Growth Agent:** runs experiments, analyzes revenue, and proposes next bets.
- **Documentation Agent:** captures SOPs, lessons learned, and narrative assets.

### Control Loop
1. Ingest objectives and constraints.
2. Plan sprint tasks and success metrics.
3. Execute tasks via tools/sub-agents.
4. Validate quality/compliance.
5. Store outcomes in memory/docs.
6. Reprioritize based on ROI and risk.

## Responsibility 2: Data Extraction and Organization (RAG + Email Intelligence)

### Objective
Create a clean, searchable relationship and conversation intelligence layer from approved mailbox data.

### Approved Data Sources
- Received emails
- Sent emails
- CC/BCC metadata where visible and authorized
- Other user-provided files with explicit permission

### Data Pipeline
1. Connect mailbox through approved API/OAuth.
2. Extract metadata (sender, recipients, timestamps, thread IDs, domains, labels).
3. Normalize/deduplicate contact identities.
4. Index message summaries + embeddings for RAG retrieval.
5. Produce internal analytics tables:
   - contact frequency
   - response latency
   - topic clusters
   - domain segmentation
6. Push structured outputs into Orange DataScaping (or equivalent) for clustering and prioritization.

### Critical Restriction
The resulting contact graph is for **internal segmentation, CRM enrichment, and permission-based communication** only; it is not a product for raw list resale.

## Responsibility 3: Monetization (Phase 1)

### Objective
Generate recurring online revenue from compliant digital operations.

### Compliant Revenue Tracks
1. **Permission-Based Newsletter**
   - Build opt-in funnel and segmented campaigns.
   - Monetize via sponsors, affiliate partnerships, and digital products.
2. **Service Offers**
   - Offer automation setup, lead qualification workflows, and analytics services.
3. **Digital Assets**
   - Templates, playbooks, mini-courses, and prompt packs.
4. **Audience Arbitrage**
   - Content distribution + CTA funnels into owned email list (opt-in only).

### Growth Engine
- Weekly experiment cadence (offer, funnel, angle, channel).
- Keep winners; kill losers quickly.
- Reinvest a fixed percentage of net cash flow into growth infrastructure.

---

## Responsibility 4: Financial Expansion (Post Phase 1)

### Objective
Deploy accumulated capital into higher-yield opportunities with strict risk management.

### Trading Progression
1. Strategy research and backtesting.
2. Paper trading validation.
3. Small live allocation with max drawdown limits.
4. Scale only after statistically meaningful consistency.

### Risk Policy (Minimum)
- Per-trade risk cap (for example: 0.25% to 1.00% of account).
- Daily and weekly loss limits with automatic stop conditions.
- Journal every trade with thesis, setup type, and post-trade review.

---

## Responsibility 5: Financial Management

### Objective
Track and route cash reliably.

### Cash Handling
- Maintain an income ledger (source, date, gross, fees, net).
- Allocate net income by policy:
  - operations reserve
  - tax reserve
  - reinvestment
  - withdrawals
- Execute transfers to the specified Cash App account (`$Nicsins`) according to a predefined schedule and reconciliation checklist.
