# Agentic Financial System — Mission Blueprint (Compliant Version)

## Program Objective

Build a self-sustaining online income system that compounds capital through lawful digital ventures, disciplined reinvestment, and transparent documentation.

## Scope Note

The original mission language included collecting and selling email lists from inbox data. That behavior is out of scope for this program because it introduces legal, privacy, and platform-policy risk. This blueprint uses consent-first acquisition and compliant monetization.

## Phase Plan

### Phase 0 (Week 1-2): Foundation and Guardrails

Deliverables:
- Compliance policy adoption (`docs/policies/compliance_pack.md`).
- Data map of approved sources and prohibited sources.
- Revenue scoreboard and weekly operating cadence.

KPIs:
- 100% workflows mapped to lawful basis.
- Daily financial ledger active.
- Weekly review ceremony running.

### Phase 1 (Week 2-6): Ethical Lead Engine and Service Revenue

Goal: generate first recurring cashflow using opt-in channels.

Workstreams:
1. Offer stack
   - Productized micro-service (automation setup, prompt engineering package, listing optimization, or workflow audits).
   - Lightweight digital product (checklist/template pack).
2. Consent-based lead capture
   - Landing page + lead magnet + double opt-in newsletter.
   - CRM pipeline with tags for source, intent, and consent timestamp.
3. Distribution system
   - Content flywheel: short posts, tutorials, and case snippets.
   - Partner channels: communities and newsletter swaps.

KPIs:
- 100+ opt-in contacts.
- First 5-10 paid customers.
- Positive weekly cashflow after core tooling costs.

### Phase 2 (Week 6-12): Systematize and Scale

Goal: shift from ad hoc tasks to repeatable automation loops.

Workstreams:
- Standard operating procedures for outreach, fulfillment, and follow-up.
- Automation for pipeline stages, reporting, and customer success touchpoints.
- Improve conversion metrics with A/B tests.

KPIs:
- 2+ acquisition channels producing leads.
- 20%+ lead-to-call or lead-to-trial conversion (target range, tune by niche).
- 30%+ repeat/referral contribution to monthly revenue.

### Phase 3 (Post-Validation): Capital Expansion (Trading Sandbox First)

Goal: test capital deployment discipline before scaling real-money exposure.

Workstreams:
- Paper-trading track record with predefined strategy rules.
- Risk playbook (position sizing, max drawdown, kill-switch conditions).
- Weekly strategy review and false-signal analysis.

KPIs:
- Minimum sample size for strategy evaluation reached before live capital.
- Rule adherence >95%.
- Drawdown within policy threshold.

## Agent Responsibilities (Program-Adaptive)

1. **Apex Orchestrator**
   - Sets weekly priorities and enforces phase gates.
   - Approves resource allocation and pauses non-compliant workflows.
2. **Toolsmith / Framework Engineer**
   - Builds reusable tools and instrument scripts.
   - Maintains observability and runbooks.
3. **Data Steward**
   - Enforces provenance, consent records, and retention/deletion controls.
   - Produces compliant contact segmentation for CRM only.
4. **Revenue Operator**
   - Runs offer tests, outreach cadence, and conversion optimization.
5. **Risk and Compliance Governor**
   - Executes pre-flight checklist and blocks unsafe actions.
6. **Content Producer**
   - Turns process logs into tutorials/course assets and narrative media.

## RAG + Email Data (Allowed Design)

Permitted pattern:
- Use RAG over the account owner's authorized mailbox exports/API scopes.
- Extract entities for:
  - relationship mapping,
  - inbox triage,
  - personal CRM enrichment,
  - follow-up prioritization.

Mandatory controls:
- Explicit labels: `source`, `consent_status`, `allowed_use`.
- Purpose binding: no repurposing contacts beyond consented use.
- Audit trail for every bulk operation.

## Monetization Channels (Compliant)

- Services: automation implementation, AI workflow consulting, ops optimization.
- Products: templates, prompts, SOP bundles, mini-courses.
- Affiliate/partnerships: only transparent and disclosed relationships.
- Community monetization: paid cohorts or support communities with clear terms.

## Content/Course Narrative Pipeline

1. Capture weekly wins/losses from journal and telemetry.
2. Convert into:
   - tutorial notes,
   - module scripts,
   - short-form clips.
3. Build a serialized story arc with an anthropomorphic narrator and milestone-driven episodes.
4. Publish with transparent disclaimers and performance evidence.

## 30-60-90 Day Outcomes

- **Day 30:** compliance-complete stack, offer live, first opt-in pipeline stable.
- **Day 60:** repeatable acquisition + fulfillment loops, documented SOPs, growing audience.
- **Day 90:** consistent revenue baseline, paper-trading validation complete, course content backlog ready for production.
