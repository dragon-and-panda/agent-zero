# Agentic Financial System - Mission Blueprint

## 1) Mission

Create a self-sustaining online financial system using autonomous agents, repeatable online business operations, and disciplined capital management.

## 2) Responsibility Mapping (Execution Version)

### A. Develop an Agentic Framework
- Build a modular control loop: intake -> research -> execution -> QA -> telemetry -> retrospectives.
- Use specialized sub-agents for lead research, product publishing, analytics, finance ops, and content production.
- Encode weekly adaptive behavior rules from KPI performance (conversion, margin, drawdown, velocity).

### B. Data Extraction (RAG + Email Intelligence)
- Connect only to mailbox data you are authorized to process.
- Build RAG indexes for:
  - Received messages
  - Sent messages
  - CC/BCC metadata
  - Related project files
- Produce only **consent-statused** contact intelligence:
  - "opted_in", "existing_customer", "partner", "unknown", "do_not_contact"
- Use Orange DataScaping for segmentation, de-duplication, enrichment checks, and campaign cohorting.

### C. Monetization - Phase 1
- Do not sell raw email lists.
- Monetize through compliant channels:
  1. Productized research reports
  2. Newsletter sponsorship inventory (opt-in audience)
  3. Affiliate offers for relevant tools/services
  4. Lead-gen services for clients who own the consent layer
  5. Micro-SaaS workflows built from recurring internal automations

### D. Financial Expansion - Post Phase 1
- Start with paper trading and strategy validation.
- Move to low-risk live deployment only after objective gates:
  - Positive expectancy over sample threshold
  - Max drawdown below defined cap
  - Risk-per-trade and daily stop discipline
- Begin with Forex only if compliance, broker, and jurisdiction constraints are satisfied.

### E. Financial Management
- Maintain a ledger of gross revenue, net profit, tax reserve, and reinvestment allocation.
- Route owner distributions to Cash App account `$Nicsins` as a manual controlled payout workflow.
- Record payout confirmations in the mission journal.

### F. Content Creation
- Capture process artifacts continuously (prompts, workflows, metrics, lessons).
- Publish a tutorial/course from real operating procedures.
- Produce a YouTube narrative arc featuring an anthropomorphic character on a mission to fund a mech suit + robot body, while keeping claims factual and non-deceptive.

## 3) Compliance Guardrails

1. No unauthorized personal data collection or resale.
2. No spam or anti-platform-ToS automation.
3. Respect CAN-SPAM/GDPR/CCPA and local laws for every campaign.
4. Store consent provenance and suppression lists.
5. Require human approval for:
   - Fund withdrawals/payouts
   - New trading account activation
   - Any high-risk acquisition channel

## 4) 90-Day Rollout Plan

### Phase 0 (Week 1-2): Foundation
- Finalize SOPs, schemas, and compliance tags.
- Implement telemetry and daily KPI snapshots.
- Stand up RAG ingestion from authorized sources.

### Phase 1 (Week 3-6): Revenue Engine
- Launch first two compliant monetization channels.
- Ship recurring outbound/inbound growth loops using only consented audiences.
- Target first stable monthly recurring revenue baseline.

### Phase 2 (Week 7-10): Scale + Optimization
- Expand channel mix and improve funnel conversion with A/B tests.
- Productize top-performing services into templatized offers.
- Add strict budget controls and CAC/LTV monitoring.

### Phase 3 (Week 11-13): Trading Sandbox
- Deploy paper-trading strategy pipeline with journaling, backtests, and risk dashboards.
- Evaluate go/no-go for limited live capital deployment.

## 5) KPI Stack

- Audience quality: opt-in growth rate, unsubscribe rate, complaint rate.
- Monetization: gross revenue, net margin, MRR growth, channel concentration risk.
- Operations: automation success rate, cycle time, task completion SLA.
- Trading (sandbox/live): win rate, expectancy, max drawdown, Sharpe proxy.
- Governance: policy violations, blocked actions, audit completeness.

## 6) Hourly Automation Cadence (Cron-Compatible)

1. Ingest new artifacts (email metadata, campaign metrics, ledgers).
2. Re-score opportunities and active experiments.
3. Update backlog priorities.
4. Write a journal entry with:
   - What changed
   - KPI deltas
   - What to do next
5. Trigger only safe autonomous tasks; queue sensitive actions for approval.

## 7) Definition of "On Track"

- Revenue channels produce repeatable positive net returns.
- Compliance incidents stay at zero.
- Documentation remains current enough for a third party to replay the system.
- Capital deployment remains within risk limits at all times.
