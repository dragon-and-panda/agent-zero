# Agentic Financial System — Mission Blueprint

This blueprint operationalizes the mission to build a self-sustaining online financial system using autonomous agents, RAG workflows, and disciplined capital allocation.

## 1) Mission Objective

Build a repeatable, legal, and scalable income engine that:
- generates online revenue from digital services/products,
- compounds gains through measured reinvestment,
- documents every step for training content and storytelling.

## 2) Non-Negotiable Guardrails

1. **No trafficking in personal data.** Do not sell scraped or non-consensual email lists.
2. **Consent-first contact model.** Only use addresses from explicit opt-ins or lawful business contacts with compliant outreach.
3. **Regulatory compliance by default.** Enforce CAN-SPAM/GDPR/CCPA requirements, suppression lists, and audit logs.
4. **Risk caps for capital deployment.** No live trading without paper-trading proof and predefined drawdown controls.
5. **Full observability.** Every automation must write logs, metrics, and decision traces.

## 3) Agentic Framework (Core System)

### 3.1 Role Topology
- **Executive Orchestrator**: sets weekly objectives and budget envelopes.
- **Data Steward Agent**: owns ingestion, consent tracking, and data quality.
- **Offer/Monetization Agent**: builds and tests revenue offers.
- **Trading Research Agent**: backtests and validates strategies.
- **Treasury Agent**: tracks cashflow, transfers, and reconciliations.
- **Media Agent**: produces tutorial/course/video artifacts.

### 3.2 Control Loop
1. Plan weekly objective and KPI targets.
2. Run ingestion and enrichment jobs.
3. Execute monetization experiments.
4. Allocate capital per treasury policy.
5. Publish retrospective + content update.

## 4) Data Extraction and RAG (Compliant Implementation)

### 4.1 Email Data Sources
- Gmail (authorized account only) via OAuth.
- Sent/received/cc metadata for relationship mapping.
- Attachments and notes only where access rights are verified.

### 4.2 Pipeline
1. **Ingest** message metadata and content with source tags.
2. **Normalize** email identities (lowercase, trim, deduplicate).
3. **Classify consent status** (`opt_in`, `business_legitimate_interest`, `unknown`, `do_not_contact`).
4. **Index into RAG store** for retrieval and analytics.
5. **Export approved segments** for outreach with provenance fields.

### 4.3 Orange DataScaping Usage
- Build segment views by industry, intent, recency, and engagement.
- Produce quality reports: duplicate rate, bounce risk, consent coverage.
- Keep a suppression list synchronized in every export step.

## 5) Monetization — Phase 1 (Legal Alternatives)

Use contact intelligence to sell **services/products**, not raw personal data.

Primary tracks:
1. **B2B outbound service**: lead qualification + appointment setting for local businesses.
2. **Newsletter sponsorship**: grow opt-in audience, then sell sponsorship inventory.
3. **Digital products**: templates, automations, and SOP packs.
4. **Affiliate/content funnels**: compliant email nurture sequences with unsubscribe handling.

Success metrics:
- Revenue/week
- Qualified meetings/week
- Conversion rate by segment
- Complaint/unsubscribe rates (must stay below policy thresholds)

## 6) Financial Expansion (Post Phase 1)

### 6.1 Readiness Gates
- Minimum 3 profitable months from core business.
- Emergency reserve funded before live trading.
- Strategy passes walk-forward backtests and paper trading.

### 6.2 Forex Progression
1. Strategy research and hypothesis registry.
2. Backtesting + forward testing.
3. Paper-trading for at least one full market regime cycle.
4. Live deployment with strict controls:
   - max risk/trade: 0.5% to 1.0%
   - daily loss stop
   - max portfolio drawdown threshold

## 7) Treasury and Cash Management

- Maintain a weekly P&L and cashflow ledger.
- Define transfer policy and reconciliation checklist.
- Route net proceeds to designated destination account (Cash App: `$Nicsins`) only after tax/operating reserve allocation and compliance checks.

## 8) Content and Narrative Engine

Deliverables each sprint:
1. **Process log**: what was run, results, blockers, next actions.
2. **Tutorial module**: one teachable workflow per sprint.
3. **Video adaptation brief**: hook, beats, visuals, CTA.
4. **Character narrative**: anthropomorphic host chronicles progress toward a mech-suit and robot-body build fund.

## 9) Initial 30/60/90 Plan

### Day 0-30
- Stand up ingestion + consent classification + RAG indexing.
- Launch first compliant outreach offer and opt-in funnel.
- Publish first tutorial episode + mission diary entries.

### Day 31-60
- Optimize offer conversion and segment quality.
- Add second revenue channel (digital product or sponsorship lane).
- Start trading strategy research notebook and paper account tracking.

### Day 61-90
- Scale proven channel with automation and QA.
- Finalize trading readiness checklist.
- Produce long-form course outline + YouTube narrative arc.

## 10) Definition of Done

- System runs on a weekly loop with measurable KPIs.
- Outreach is fully consent-aware with auditable logs.
- Revenue is recurring from at least one validated channel.
- Mission diary and tutorial assets are continuously updated.
