# Agentic Financial System — Mission Charter

## 1) Mission
Build a self-sustaining, legally compliant online financial system that can fund long-term goals through repeatable digital ventures, disciplined capital allocation, and transparent operations.

## 2) Non-Negotiable Guardrails
- No unauthorized data access.
- No selling scraped, purchased, or non-consensual personal data.
- No spam workflows or deceptive outreach.
- No promises of guaranteed trading profits.
- Follow applicable privacy and communications rules (for example: GDPR/CCPA/CAN-SPAM equivalents in target markets).
- Keep auditable records of data provenance, consent status, and campaign usage.

## 3) Agent Responsibilities (Operationalized)

### A. Develop the Agentic Framework
- Build modular agents for:
  - Data ingestion and normalization
  - Contact intelligence and deduplication
  - Opportunity scoring
  - Revenue experiment execution
  - Risk/compliance monitoring
  - Reporting and content generation
- Require each module to emit logs, metrics, and retry-safe outputs.

### B. Data Extraction and RAG (Consent-First)
- Use Gmail API or Google Takeout only with explicit account authorization.
- Extract and normalize contact signals from:
  - From / To / CC / Reply-To fields
  - Signature blocks
  - User-approved files and exports
- Attach metadata to each contact:
  - source
  - timestamp
  - confidence score
  - consent/legal basis tag
- Index approved data for RAG-based retrieval and QA.
- Use Orange DataScaping (or equivalent workflow) for:
  - deduplication
  - clustering
  - segmentation
  - quality scoring

### C. Monetization (Phase 1)
Focus on legitimate, consent-based revenue channels:
- Permission-based newsletter and sponsorship pipeline
- Affiliate content + productized research reports
- Service-based outreach where recipients are opted-in or sourced from public business directories with lawful basis
- Audience growth via educational content and lead magnets

### D. Financial Expansion (Post Phase 1)
- Build a capital allocation policy before live trading:
  - emergency reserve target
  - max risk per trade
  - max drawdown limits
  - stop-trading conditions
- Treat Forex as an experimental strategy lane, beginning with:
  - paper trading
  - backtests
  - tiny position sizing
- Expand only after statistically meaningful performance and risk stability.

### E. Financial Management
- Define a settlement SOP:
  1. Reconcile venture revenue daily/weekly.
  2. Move net funds to operating account.
  3. Transfer approved amount to Cash App account `$Nicsins` on cadence.
  4. Record transfer IDs and balances in the ledger.
- Keep tax and compliance bookkeeping up to date.

### F. Content Creation
- Document architecture, experiments, wins, failures, and lessons.
- Produce tutorial/course assets from real workflows.
- Convert course material into a YouTube series with an anthropomorphic narrator.
- Anchor the narrative arc around funding milestones toward:
  - a mech suit goal
  - a robot body goal

## 4) Phased Roadmap

### Phase 0 — Foundation (Weeks 1-2)
- Set up data model, consent tags, and logging.
- Implement ingestion + RAG prototype.
- Define compliance checks and fail-safe blocks.

### Phase 1 — Revenue Engine (Weeks 3-8)
- Launch 2-3 revenue experiments.
- Track conversion, CAC, retention, and net margin.
- Standardize the best-performing funnel.

### Phase 2 — Capital Growth (Weeks 9+)
- Start paper-trading and strategy validation.
- Deploy controlled live trading under strict risk caps.
- Reinvest by policy, not emotion.

### Phase 3 — Media Flywheel (Continuous)
- Weekly publish cadence for tutorial/video outputs.
- Build audience-owned channels (email + community).
- Recycle learnings into product and service offers.

## 5) KPI Dashboard
- MRR and cash runway
- Qualified leads (consent-verified)
- Funnel conversion rate
- Net profit margin
- Trading Sharpe-like risk-adjusted metrics
- Max drawdown
- Weekly content output
- Audience growth rate

## 6) Definition of Success
- System operates autonomously with minimal manual intervention.
- Revenue is diversified across multiple lawful channels.
- Risk remains bounded by policy.
- Documentation and media outputs are consistent and reusable.
