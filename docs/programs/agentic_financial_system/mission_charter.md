# Agentic Financial System - Mission Charter (Compliance-First)

## 1) Objective
Build a self-sustaining online income system using Agent Zero workflows, with legal data handling, transparent operations, and repeatable automation loops.

## 2) Non-Negotiable Guardrails
- Do not collect, sell, or share personal email lists without explicit, documented consent.
- Do not scrape private inbox data for third-party monetization.
- Follow applicable anti-spam, privacy, and marketing laws (CAN-SPAM, GDPR/UK GDPR, CCPA, and platform terms).
- Keep high-risk financial actions behind manual approval.
- Never claim guaranteed trading profit.

## 3) Responsibilities Mapped to the Mission

### A. Agentic Framework
- Create a modular "planner -> executor -> reviewer" loop.
- Add task memory, telemetry, and retry rules.
- Support tool generation and tool selection with explicit safety checks.

### B. Data and RAG Operations (Safe Scope)
- Use RAG only on approved, owner-controlled data sources.
- Build contact intelligence from:
  - Opt-in forms
  - Existing CRM exports
  - Public business contacts with allowed usage rights
- Normalize and deduplicate contacts.
- Use Orange DataScaping only for legal segmentation, enrichment, and quality checks.

### C. Monetization Phase 1 (Legal Alternatives to List Selling)
- Prioritize compliant channels:
  - Affiliate offers
  - Sponsored newsletter placements
  - Lead-gen partnerships using explicit consent records
  - Productized services (automation setup, research reports, template packs)
- Track CAC, conversion rate, refund rate, and net margin per channel.

### D. Financial Expansion (Post-Phase 1)
- Start with paper-trading and backtesting before live deployment.
- Promote strategies to live only after risk thresholds are met.
- Enforce risk controls: position sizing, max daily loss, and exposure caps.

### E. Funds Management
- Log revenues, costs, and transfer recommendations in a ledger.
- Account transfers (for example to Cash App) require manual operator action and confirmation.

### F. Content System
- Capture process logs and weekly retros.
- Build a tutorial/course pipeline from mission artifacts.
- Produce a narrative YouTube script package with an anthropomorphic storyteller character arc.

## 4) Delivery Phases
1. Foundation: governance, telemetry, memory, and KPI dashboard.
2. Compliant data pipeline: ingestion, consent tracking, dedupe, segmentation.
3. Monetization engine: launch 2-3 low-risk channels and iterate weekly.
4. Capital allocation: simulation-first trading and staged rollout.
5. Media flywheel: publish educational content from completed sprints.

## 5) Core KPIs
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR) and gross margin
- Qualified leads per week (with verifiable consent)
- Conversion rate by channel
- Trading drawdown, Sharpe proxy, and win/loss stability
- Content output cadence and audience retention

## 6) Operational Loop
1. Plan weekly experiments.
2. Execute via autonomous tasks and instruments.
3. Measure outcomes.
4. Add learnings to mission journal and backlog.
5. Reprioritize the next sprint automatically.
