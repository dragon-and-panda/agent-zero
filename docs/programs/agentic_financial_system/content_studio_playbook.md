# Agentic Financial System — Content Studio Playbook

This playbook converts execution data into educational assets and story-driven media.

---

## 1. Output Stack Per Week

1. **Operations Report (internal)**
   - KPI changes
   - Experiments run
   - Wins, losses, and hypotheses

2. **Tutorial Module (course asset)**
   - Problem statement
   - Setup steps
   - Execution walkthrough
   - Results and troubleshooting

3. **YouTube Episode Script**
   - Hook
   - Build log
   - Lesson
   - Next mission teaser

4. **Short-form Clips (3-5)**
   - Single insight per clip
   - One CTA per clip (lead magnet, newsletter, or course waitlist)

---

## 2. Character Design: "Forge"

- **Role:** Anthropomorphic narrator and mission commander.
- **Core motive:** Earn enough to construct a mech suit and a full robot body.
- **Personality:** Determined, strategic, transparent about mistakes.
- **Visual motifs:** Blueprint overlays, upgrade tree, progress meter ("Mech Fund").

### Story Rule
Every episode must tie narrative progress to real business metrics.

---

## 3. Episode Template (8-12 Minutes)

1. **Cold Open (0:00-0:30)**
   - "Current mech fund" and mission target.
2. **Challenge Brief (0:30-1:30)**
   - What bottleneck is being attacked this week.
3. **Execution (1:30-6:00)**
   - Data, automation, campaign actions.
4. **Results (6:00-8:00)**
   - KPI shifts, revenue impact, and what failed.
5. **Lesson + Next Step (8:00-10:00)**
   - Actionable takeaway and next mission objective.

---

## 4. Compliance for Content

- No sharing private personal data.
- No unverifiable income claims.
- Include risk disclaimers for financial/trading content.
- Ensure all outreach examples are consent-based and unsubscribe-ready.
