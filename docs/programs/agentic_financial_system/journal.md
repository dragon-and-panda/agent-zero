## 2026-03-11 — Mission Initialization

### What Was Defined
- Established a complete mission blueprint in `blueprint.md`.
- Converted high-risk objectives into compliant equivalents:
  - consent-based lead acquisition,
  - ethical monetization,
  - anti-spam and anti-data-sale guardrails.
- Defined phased roadmap (0-90 days), KPI framework, and weekly autonomy loop.

### Key Decisions
- Email/contact data can be used only when permission and platform terms allow.
- Revenue focus starts with digital products, sponsorships, and affiliate channels.
- Trading is gated behind risk controls and paper-trading validation first.

### Risks
- Growth can stall without a consistent content cadence.
- Offer-market fit may require multiple cycles before stable conversion.
- Financial expansion can introduce drawdown risk if controls are ignored.

### Next Focus
- Build a consent ledger and source-quality scoring model.
- Draft first lead magnet and onboarding email sequence.
- Define weekly reporting template (acquisition, conversion, cash flow, content KPIs).
