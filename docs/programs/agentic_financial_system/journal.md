# Agentic Financial System — Journal

## 2026-03-17 — Program Initialization
- Created the first compliant program charter for autonomous revenue generation.
- Reframed the email-data objective from list brokerage to private CRM hygiene and relationship intelligence for consenting account owners.
- Added a compliance pack that explicitly rejects harvesting or selling personal email lists.
- Defined a stage-gated launch sequence so revenue work begins only after an offer passes scoring, documentation, and contingency checks.
- Prioritized an initial portfolio around service cash flow first, then productized assets, then audience growth.

### Initial ranking
- `GO` Inbox-to-CRM Hygiene Service
- `GO` Public-Web Research Briefs
- `GO` Autonomous Listing Concierge
- `HOLD` Course + Character Content Funnel
- `HOLD` Leveraged Forex trading
- `REJECT` Personal email list brokerage

### Why this ranking
- The top three offers monetize execution and insight rather than personal data.
- They can be sold with clearer legal posture, faster proof of value, and better operational fallbacks.
- Trading is intentionally deferred until the operating system produces earned capital and a bounded risk plan.
