# Agentic Financial System - Mission Diary

> Tracks how the revenue system is assembled, tested, and revised over time.

## 2026-03-17 - Program Reset and Safe Revenue Baseline

- Reframed the mission away from personal-email extraction and contact-list resale.
- Added a formal compliance pack for privacy, consent, outreach, and capital-risk guardrails.
- Established the core program charter with a diversified revenue stack:
  - fast-cash services,
  - reusable tooling/templates,
  - compounding content,
  - delayed, tightly controlled capital experiments.
- Created a strategy intake queue and a scoring instrument so future ideas are filtered by legality, consent quality, resilience, and margin before launch.

### Metrics / Signals

- Safe data-use policy now exists for any inbox or CRM automation.
- Revenue activation gate now requires an offer, intake path, fulfillment checklist, and at least one backup acquisition channel.
- Trading is explicitly gated behind simulation, reserves, and drawdown controls.

### Next Focus

- Convert the top two opportunities in the intake queue into concrete go-to-market packs.
- Create a simple landing page or demo flow for the lead offer.
- Add telemetry or a lightweight report format for tracking revenue, reserves, and channel concentration.
