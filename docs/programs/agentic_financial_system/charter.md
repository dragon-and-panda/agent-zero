# Agentic Financial System Charter

## Mission
Build a self-sustaining online revenue system that compounds through lawful, ethical, and automatable ventures while minimizing human touchpoints.

## Guardrails
- Follow `docs/policies/compliance_pack.md` for all acquisition, outreach, publishing, and payment workflows.
- Reject personal-data resale, scraped contact monetization, spam, and regulated financial promotions.
- Prefer first-party assets, opt-in channels, public-web research, and owned inventory or seller-authorized listings.

## Phase map

### Phase 0: Control plane
- Maintain a scored venture queue in `docs/strategy/incoming.md`.
- Use `instruments/strategy/score.sh` before investing automation effort in a new idea.
- Keep a rolling journal and improvement backlog for each active revenue lane.

### Phase 1: Immediate revenue lanes
1. Inbox-to-CRM Hygiene Service
   - Offer: organize an account owner's own inbox contacts, dedupe senders, tag relationship status, and export a compliant CRM-ready sheet.
   - Why first: low capital intensity, repeatable, fits first-party data rules.
2. Autonomous Listing Concierge
   - Offer: turn seller-owned goods into polished listings with compliance checks, publishing packages, and buyer follow-up support.
   - Why second: reuses the repo's existing service surface and can generate faster validation signals.
3. Public-Web Research Briefs
   - Offer: niche market maps, competitor tracking, and sourcing briefs based on public information.
   - Why third: broad demand, low legal risk, strong content crossover.

### Phase 2: Content flywheel
- Convert repeatable operating procedures into templates, tutorials, and a course.
- Build the narrative layer around an anthropomorphic guide character documenting the climb from basic revenue operations to advanced automation projects.
- Feed learnings from real delivery work back into the content library.

### Phase 3: Capital deployment
- Preserve an operating reserve before experimenting with higher-risk capital allocation.
- Limit trading work to simulation and research until the reserve threshold, compliance review, and drawdown rules are satisfied.

## Redundancy and contingency design
- Keep at least two active revenue lanes so one client or platform change does not halt cash flow.
- Maintain manual fallback paths for publication, fulfillment, and client communication.
- Store playbooks, offer templates, and scored opportunities in versioned docs so later runs can resume without hidden context.
- Escalate any blocked or unclear monetization idea back through the scoring queue instead of improvising around policy limits.

## Immediate operating plan
When baseline infrastructure and prompts are ready:
1. Score the top three compliant ideas.
2. Move the highest-scoring GO idea into active execution.
3. Keep the second GO idea warm as a hedge if the primary lane stalls.
4. Document every win, blocker, and policy learning in the program journal.
