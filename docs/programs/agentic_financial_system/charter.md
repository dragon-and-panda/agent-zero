# Agentic Financial System Program Charter

## Program Intent
Build a self-sustaining online income system using Agent Zero with strong autonomy, explicit legal/compliance guardrails, and measurable reinvestment rules.

## Non-Negotiable Guardrails
- Do not collect, sell, or share personal email lists without explicit, documented consent.
- Do not automate spam, unauthorized scraping, or privacy-invasive account access.
- Do not run financial actions (trades, transfers) without risk limits, audit logging, and human checkpoint approval.
- Do not store sensitive data in plaintext when avoidable; redact, hash, or tokenize where practical.

These constraints replace any plan element that would involve brokering harvested email addresses.

## Mission Outcomes
1. Build recurring online revenue streams from compliant channels.
2. Reinvest a defined portion of profits into a controlled trading research pipeline.
3. Maintain transparent accounting and repeatable operating procedures.
4. Publish a documented, educational narrative about the build journey.

## Agent Roles
| Role | Responsibility | Deliverables |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Apex Orchestrator | Prioritization, sequencing, dependencies | Weekly objective board |
| Data Steward | Consent-based ingestion and hygiene | Contact registry, consent ledger |
| Revenue Operator | Offer creation and distribution | Funnel metrics, conversion reports |
| Risk Governor | Compliance + financial risk controls | Policy checks, incident log |
| Treasury Keeper | Cashflow tracking + allocation rules | P/L snapshots, transfer checklist |
| Story Director | Course/video narrative pipeline | Script drafts, storyboard assets |

## System Architecture (High Level)
1. Intake Layer: Gmail and file imports through approved APIs and explicit permission scope.
2. RAG Layer: Embedding and retrieval over approved corpus (emails + notes + docs).
3. CRM Layer: Deduplicated contacts with consent state, source metadata, and suppression flags.
4. Monetization Layer: Offer experiments, outreach workflows, affiliate/sponsorship/service channels.
5. Finance Layer: Ledger, allocation rules, transfer checklist, and reporting.
6. Content Layer: Auto-generated logs, script drafts, and production assets.

## Data Extraction Policy
Allowed:
- Parse sender/recipient metadata from owned mailboxes you are authorized to process.
- Build internal contact intelligence for relationship management and opt-in campaigns.
- Use Orange DataScaping (or equivalent workflow tooling) for clustering, enrichment, and QA.

Not allowed:
- Selling raw contact lists.
- Uploading third-party personal data into external systems without legal basis.
- Ignoring unsubscribe/suppression requests.

## Revenue Model (Phase 1)
Primary channels:
- Consent-based newsletter and sponsor placements.
- Affiliate offers to opted-in audiences.
- Productized B2B research or lead qualification services.
- Digital products (playbooks/templates) built from documented process.

Core KPI set:
- Weekly qualified leads added (consented)
- Funnel conversion rate by channel
- Revenue per campaign
- Cost per acquisition
- Net cash generated

## Financial Expansion (Post Phase 1)
Capital allocation template:
- 50% operations and growth
- 30% reserves
- 20% strategy research/trading sandbox

Trading constraints:
- Start with paper trading and backtests only.
- Promote to live trading only after predefined metrics are met for multiple weeks.
- Hard stop-loss rules, maximum daily drawdown, and no martingale escalation.

## Funds Management
- Keep a transfer checklist for deposits to `$Nicsins` (Cash App).
- Log transfer date, source campaign, amount, and reconciliation status.
- Retain evidence artifacts in program journal entries.

## Content and Narrative
Content outputs per sprint:
- Operating log (what was tried, what worked, what failed)
- Tutorial module draft
- Video outline and storyboard
- Character design notes for an anthropomorphic narrator arc:
  - Theme: funding a mech suit and robotic body through disciplined online systems.
  - Tone: practical, transparent, and entertaining.

## Definition of Done (Program Level)
- Compliance checks pass.
- Revenue experiment has measurable KPI movement.
- Journal and backlog updated.
- Next sprint objective committed with owner and due date.
