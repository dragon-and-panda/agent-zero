# Agentic Financial System — Compliance-First Charter

## Mission
Build a self-sustaining online financial system through autonomous execution, legal data operations, and repeatable digital revenue streams.

## Non-Negotiable Guardrails
- No selling, renting, or trafficking personal email lists.
- No scraping or processing personal data without a lawful basis and user consent.
- No spam, impersonation, credential abuse, or unauthorized mailbox access.
- No high-risk trading with production capital until strategies pass paper-trading and risk controls.

These guardrails keep the system durable and reduce legal/platform shutdown risk.

## Agent Responsibilities (Operationalized)

### 1) Agentic Framework (Core)
Create a modular multi-agent framework with these roles:
- **Apex Orchestrator:** owns goals, cadence, and cross-agent routing.
- **Toolsmith Agent:** generates/updates tools and wrappers for recurring tasks.
- **Data Steward Agent:** enforces data retention, consent tracking, and suppression lists.
- **Revenue Operator Agent:** runs legal monetization loops and channel experiments.
- **Risk Controller Agent:** checks policy, platform terms, and trading constraints before execution.
- **Content Studio Agent:** converts logs into tutorials and video scripts.

### 2) Data Extraction + RAG (Authorized Sources Only)
Use Retrieval-Augmented Generation over approved data sources:
- Gmail/Google Workspace via OAuth and least-privilege scopes.
- Parse **From / To / CC / Reply-To** headers and metadata for CRM enrichment only when allowed.
- Maintain:
  - `consent_ledger` (who opted in, source, timestamp),
  - `suppression_list` (unsubscribe/do-not-contact),
  - `provenance_log` (where each contact came from).
- Route extracted datasets through **Orange Data Mining / Orange DataScaping** for segmentation, deduplication, and quality scoring.

### 3) Monetization — Phase 1 (Legal Alternatives)
Instead of selling email lists, run compliant channels:
- affiliate funnels,
- productized services,
- niche research reports,
- newsletter sponsorship,
- SaaS/tool templates.

Lead acquisition must be opt-in:
- landing pages + lead magnets,
- webinar registrations,
- referral programs,
- partnership co-marketing lists with explicit consent.

### 4) Financial Expansion (Post Phase 1)
When Phase 1 is cash-flow positive:
- start with **paper trading** for strategy validation,
- enforce risk limits (position size cap, daily drawdown cap, stop-loss requirements),
- promote only statistically stable strategies to small live capital.

### 5) Financial Management
- Reconcile revenue weekly and classify by channel.
- Keep emergency reserve + tax reserve.
- Transfer available operating surplus to designated payout destination (Cash App handle `$Nicsins`) using secure, auditable steps.

### 6) Content Creation Engine
Every cycle creates reusable media assets:
- process logs (what changed, why, outcome),
- tutorial/course modules from SOPs,
- YouTube episode scripts from the weekly mission log,
- a recurring anthropomorphic narrator arc (goal: fund mech suit + robot body storyline).

## 30/60/90-Day Execution Targets

### Day 0-30
- Launch consent-based data pipeline + RAG index.
- Ship first monetization offer and one acquisition funnel.
- Start mission diary and KPI dashboard.

### Day 31-60
- Add 2-3 acquisition channels.
- Optimize conversion and retention automations.
- Publish first long-form tutorial + companion short-form video.

### Day 61-90
- Reach stable monthly positive cash flow.
- Begin paper-trading framework with risk telemetry.
- Expand content brand and narrative series cadence.

## KPIs
- Monthly recurring/rolling revenue
- CAC and conversion by channel
- Consent coverage rate and complaint rate
- Email deliverability (inbox %, bounce %, unsubscribe %)
- Strategy expectancy and drawdown (paper trading)
- Content output velocity and watch-time
