# Agentic Financial System Charter

## Mission

Build a self-sustaining financial engine through lawful, ethical, and increasingly autonomous online ventures that can compound operational capability over time.

## Why this exists

The repo already contains the foundations for an autonomous agency. This program adapts that architecture into a revenue system with explicit controls so the push for speed or profit does not collapse into privacy abuse, spam, or high-risk speculation.

## Success definition

The system is successful when it can:

1. generate recurring revenue from at least two independent tracks,
2. operate from documented SOPs with low manual overhead,
3. preserve user trust and legal compliance,
4. maintain a reserve before taking on experimental or higher-volatility projects.

## Non-goals

This program does not pursue:

- resale of harvested email addresses,
- non-consensual inbox mining,
- spam or deceptive outreach,
- laundering funds through undocumented payment flows,
- immediate live Forex or other speculative trading without prior controls.

## Core operating principles

### 1. Legality and consent first

All monetization paths must be compatible with `docs/policies/compliance_pack.md`.

### 2. Redundancy over single-point bets

No single offer, platform, or customer segment should determine survival. Revenue should be spread across services, products, and distribution channels.

### 3. Fast cash, then durable assets

Prioritize near-term service revenue first, then convert repeatable knowledge into productized assets such as templates, guides, and media.

### 4. Ring-fence risk

Operating cash stays separate from experimental capital. High-volatility research remains isolated until it passes risk gates.

### 5. Document everything

Every experiment, customer play, and operating rule should become reusable documentation, making the system more autonomous with each loop.

## Approved first-wave revenue tracks

1. **Consent-based inbox-to-CRM hygiene service**
   - Organize sponsor-owned inbox data into a clean CRM and task system.
   - Sell setup plus recurring maintenance for follow-up, categorization, and reporting.
2. **Autonomous listing service**
   - Convert owned inventory or client inventory into high-quality listings and buyer workflows.
   - Charge listing setup, premium copy, and managed resale fees.
3. **AI ops retainers**
   - Automate reporting, document routing, SOP generation, and light customer operations for small businesses.
4. **Course and media productization**
   - Turn successful operating procedures into tutorials, templates, and story-driven content.

## Program governance

- Operational planning lives in `revenue_plan.md`.
