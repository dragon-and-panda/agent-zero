# Agentic Financial System Charter

## Mission

Build a self-sustaining financial system through lawful, consent-based online ventures that can be increasingly automated inside Agent Zero with minimal human supervision.

## North-Star Outcome

Create a portfolio of small but durable revenue loops that:

- start with low capital requirements,
- compound through reusable tools and content,
- preserve customer trust,
- and avoid dependence on privacy abuse or speculative trading.

## Program Constraints

- Follow `docs/policies/compliance_pack.md` for every monetization workflow.
- Use customer-owned or public data only, with explicit authorization where required.
- Favor service-first validation before building complex products.
- Keep redundancy across at least two active revenue tracks at all times.
- Do not treat trading or leverage as the bootstrap path.

## Primary Revenue Tracks

1. **Inbox-to-CRM Hygiene Service**
   - Customer grants access to their mailbox export or account through an approved workflow.
   - System extracts contacts, threads, and routing signals only to clean, deduplicate, and enrich the customer's CRM.
   - Deliverables: clean contact graph, segmentation tags, follow-up queue, SOPs.

2. **Autonomous Listing / Resale Operations**
   - Use the listing service blueprint to turn underutilized goods into compliant marketplace listings and case studies.
   - Deliverables: listings, telemetry, response templates, resale playbooks.

3. **Content, Course, and Template Products**
   - Document each workflow, then convert it into a tutorial, prompt pack, course, and video scripts.
   - Deliverables: course modules, checklists, demo assets, lead magnets.

4. **Public-Data Research and Prospecting Support**
   - Research public businesses, verticals, and workflow pain points to feed compliant consulting or agency offers.
   - Deliverables: market maps, offer briefs, outreach assets for opted-in or warm channels.

## Non-Goals

- Email-list brokerage
- Spam automation
- Unauthorized inbox mining
- CAPTCHA/anti-bot evasion
- Directing funds to personal payment destinations without proper accounting
- Early-stage Forex or leveraged trading

## Operating Model

- **Apex Orchestrator:** Maintains the portfolio view and opportunity ranking.
- **Compliance Guardian:** Applies GO/HOLD/REJECT logic before execution.
- **Delivery Pods:** Execute one revenue loop at a time with clear SOPs and telemetry.
- **Content Pod:** Converts every proven workflow into tutorials, assets, and narrative media.

## Storytelling Layer

The public-facing content may use an anthropomorphic narrator character that frames the journey as building resources for a mech suit and robot body. The story should remain clearly fictionalized and should not obscure how revenue is actually generated.
