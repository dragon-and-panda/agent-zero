# Agentic Financial System Revenue Plan

This plan starts revenue accumulation as soon as the activation threshold in `charter.md` is satisfied. It emphasizes parallel offers, low fixed cost, and built-in fallback paths.

---

## 1. Launch Order

Run the first three offers in parallel, but weight effort toward the best-performing one after the first validation loop.

| Offer | Customer | Revenue Type | Delivery Mode | Backup Path |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Inbox-to-CRM Hygiene Service | founders, freelancers, operators | setup fee + retainer | consent-based mailbox and CRM cleanup | convert to CRM-only cleanup |
| Autonomous Listing Concierge | local sellers, flippers, resellers | per-listing fee + upsells | seller asset enhancement and listing ops | swap marketplaces or narrow to a niche |
| AI Operations Audit Pack | small business owners | fixed-fee blueprint | workflow audit plus automation roadmap | implementation sprint or template sale |

---

## 2. Operating Sequence

### Step 1 — Screen Ideas
- Put every new idea into `docs/strategy/incoming.md`.
- Score it with `instruments/strategy/score.sh`.
- Reject anything blocked by the compliance pack.

### Step 2 — Package the Offer
Each offer needs:

- one-sentence promise,
- simple scope,
- fixed first deliverable,
- price floor,
- list of upsells,
- proof artifact or demo.

### Step 3 — Build Lightweight Delivery Assets
Create:

- intake checklist,
- delivery SOP,
- final handoff template,
- case-study capture template.

### Step 4 — Start Revenue Capture
Begin with the shortest path to payment:

- setup fee before deep work,
- milestone billing for larger tasks,
- retainer only after the first clear win.

### Step 5 — Convert Delivery Into Distribution
Every completed workflow should be recycled into:

- a case study,
- a template,
- course material,
- a short-form or long-form content asset.

---

## 3. Offer Details

### 3.1 Inbox-to-CRM Hygiene Service

**Promise**
Turn a messy inbox into a usable, private relationship system for the owner of that inbox.

**Core deliverables**
- label and segment important contacts,
- identify stale follow-ups,
- generate relationship summaries,
- export clean contact data to CRM or spreadsheet,
- document a repeatable follow-up system.

**Upsells**
- CRM implementation,
- reply drafting workflows,
- simple pipeline dashboard,
- monthly maintenance retainer.

**Contingency**
If mailbox access is delayed or restricted, deliver a CRM cleanup or customer-support tagging package using exported customer data instead.

### 3.2 Autonomous Listing Concierge

**Promise**
Turn rough photos and notes into polished listings and managed sales conversations.

**Core deliverables**
- listing enhancement,
- channel-ready copy,
- platform publication,
- buyer response support,
- closeout checklist.

**Upsells**
- premium image treatment,
- cross-platform syndication,
- negotiation support,
- dropship or fulfillment integration later.

**Contingency**
If a marketplace stalls, route inventory to other channels or offer niche-specific listing optimization only.

### 3.3 AI Operations Audit Pack

**Promise**
Show a business where automation will save time or create revenue, then hand them a clear implementation plan.

**Core deliverables**
- process inventory,
- bottleneck map,
- ranked automation opportunities,
- recommended tool stack,
- implementation roadmap.

**Upsells**
- implementation sprint,
- ongoing optimization retainer,
- staff training,
- documentation package.

**Contingency**
If implementation is not approved, sell templates and SOPs as a lower-ticket follow-on.

## 4. Capital Allocation Rules

Use earned funds in this order:

1. operating expenses,
2. reserve buffer,
3. tooling and data infrastructure,
4. distribution and proof-building,
5. controlled experiments.

Speculative trading should stay in research mode until the reserve and operating process are both stable.

---

## 5. Story and Content Layer

The educational content system should document the real operating journey:

- what offers were tested,
- what failed and why,
- what the system automated,
- how losses were limited,
- how assets were reinvested.

The anthropomorphic narrator and mech-suit story can serve as a recurring content motif that makes the material memorable while still reflecting real process data.

---

## 6. Failure Handling

- If an offer underperforms, do not add complexity immediately; tighten positioning first.
- If fulfillment becomes manual-heavy, convert the noisy steps into scripts or templates before scaling.
- If a data source or platform is lost, shift volume to another offer rather than forcing the broken channel.
- If cash flow weakens, pause nonessential experiments and protect the reserve.

The program should always preserve enough optionality to keep operating even if one acquisition channel, tool vendor, or offer fails.
