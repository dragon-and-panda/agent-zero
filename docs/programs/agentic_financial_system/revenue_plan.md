# Agentic Financial System Revenue Plan

This plan turns the charter into an execution sequence that prioritizes lawful cash flow, redundancy, and low operational fragility.

---

## 1. Phase Structure

### Phase 0: Controls First

Before revenue activation:

- Adopt the compliance pack
- Score each initiative with `instruments/strategy/score.sh`
- Record the decision in `docs/strategy/incoming.md`
- Create a source registry and simple bookkeeping workflow

### Phase 1: Immediate Revenue Accumulation

Focus on service and product offers that can launch without risky capital deployment:

1. Consent-based Inbox-to-CRM Hygiene Service
2. Autonomous Listing Operations
3. Content, templates, and course assets

### Phase 2: Expansion

After recurring revenue exists:

- Add higher-ticket retainers
- Productize delivery artifacts
- Expand into partnerships and sponsorships

### Phase 3: Treasury Research

Only after stable revenue:

- Run paper trading and scenario testing
- Treat trading as capital management research, not the main engine

---

## 2. Revenue Tracks

## Track A: Inbox-to-CRM Hygiene Service

### Offer

Turn a client's owned inbox or exports into a clean, tagged CRM asset without reselling any contact data.

### Workflow

1. Client authorizes access via OAuth or supplies CSV exports.
2. System extracts only business-relevant entities and preserves provenance.
3. Orange Data Mining is used for deduplication, clustering, and segment review.
4. RAG layer consults the client's SOPs and prior campaign history.
5. Deliver CRM export, suggested segments, and next-step playbook.

### Monetization

- One-time setup fee
- Monthly hygiene retainer
- Optional campaign-readiness add-on

### Contingency

- If API access is delayed, switch to manual CSV onboarding.
- If compliance evidence is incomplete, pause the job and request clearer authorization.

---

## Track B: Autonomous Listing Operations

### Offer

Use the repo's listing pipeline to create, publish, and support listings for owned or client-authorized inventory.

### Workflow

1. Intake product photos and notes.
2. Enhance images and generate marketplace-tailored copy.
3. Publish to supported channels.
4. Monitor replies and route approved responses.
5. Track sell-through and feed learnings back into the program.

### Monetization

- Listing package fee
- Ongoing marketplace operations retainer
- Revenue share where contractually appropriate

### Contingency

- If one marketplace throttles or underperforms, pivot to others and preserve leads in the shared CRM.
- If demand is seasonal, repurpose the content engine to attract sellers in adjacent categories.

---

## Track C: Content and Course Engine

### Offer

Document the journey and package operating knowledge into media and digital products.

### Content Pillars

- Building a lawful autonomous business stack
- Operations diaries and postmortems
- Templates, checklists, and scored opportunity teardowns
- Narrative videos following an anthropomorphic character raising funds for a mech suit and robot body

### Monetization

- Course sales
- Prompt packs and SOP bundles
- Sponsorships
- Affiliate revenue with disclosure

### Contingency

- If long-form video is slow to produce, cut each lesson into short-form clips and newsletter issues.
- If one format stalls, convert the same material into templates and workshops.

---

## Track D: Tooling and Templates

### Offer

Package repeatable delivery components from Tracks A-C.

### Examples

- Intake checklists
- Proposal generators
- Opportunity scorecards
- CRM cleanup playbooks
- Listing QA worksheets

### Monetization

- Template bundles
- Membership library
- Consulting accelerators

---

## 3. Redundancy Design

The system should not depend on one buyer source, one platform, or one offer.

- **Demand redundancy:** inbound content, direct proposals, marketplace exposure, referrals
- **Revenue redundancy:** services, digital products, affiliates, sponsorships
- **Operational redundancy:** OAuth and CSV import paths, multiple marketplaces, more than one content format
- **Knowledge redundancy:** every successful workflow is converted into reusable SOPs and templates

---

## 4. Immediate Launch Sequence

Once the minimum controls exist, activate in this order:

1. Publish the compliant offer stack and intake rubric.
2. Pilot one Inbox-to-CRM engagement and one listing engagement.
3. Document every step in the mission journal.
4. Convert recurring steps into templates.
5. Release content explaining the system and lessons learned.

This sequence starts revenue accumulation quickly while building reusable assets at the same time.

---

## 5. Hold List

Do not activate yet:

- Live Forex or leveraged trading
- Unconsented inbox mining
- Selling contact lists
- Automatic transfers to personal payment handles

These remain outside the approved early-stage workflow.
