# Financial Autonomy Program — Mission Charter

This charter translates the financial autonomy objective into a legal, durable, and automation-friendly operating plan.

---

## 1. Mission
- Build a self-sustaining online income system that compounds into larger capital pools.
- Operate through an agentic framework that can plan, execute, measure, and improve with minimal human intervention.
- Keep the system compliant with privacy, anti-spam, and financial regulations.

---

## 2. Non-Negotiable Guardrails
- **No unauthorized data access:** only process accounts and files the operator explicitly owns or has permission to use.
- **No selling private email lists:** do not extract/sell personal contact data from inboxes or private sources.
- **Consent-first outreach:** contacts must come from opt-in forms, direct consent, or clearly public B2B records where outreach is allowed.
- **Compliance by default:** enforce CAN-SPAM, GDPR/UK GDPR, CCPA (as applicable), and platform terms.
- **Trading safety:** no live leveraged trading until strategy passes simulator + paper trading gates.

---

## 3. Agent Responsibilities (Compliant Version)

### 3.1 Agentic Framework Development
- Build modular agents for:
  - acquisition strategy
  - data quality and enrichment
  - offer creation and testing
  - financial control and risk management
  - documentation and content production
- Maintain role prompts and SOPs in versioned docs.

### 3.2 Data Pipeline and RAG
- Connect to Google Workspace/Gmail with OAuth and explicit user permission.
- Use RAG to answer business questions from owned communications and notes.
- Build contact intelligence from lawful sources:
  - sent/received headers from owned inboxes
  - CRM exports
  - website opt-ins
  - public business directories with allowed usage
- Normalize and deduplicate records; track consent status per contact.
- Use Orange DataScaping for clustering, segmentation, and campaign analysis.

### 3.3 Monetization — Phase 1 (Low-Risk, Legal)
- Prioritize revenue channels that do not require reselling personal data:
  - opt-in newsletter products
  - B2B lead research as a service (public/compliant sources only)
  - affiliate offers and digital products
  - done-for-you outreach infrastructure for clients
- Run weekly experiment loops:
  - hypothesis -> launch -> measure -> keep/kill
- Store KPI snapshots in the mission journal.

### 3.4 Financial Expansion (After Stable Cash Flow)
- Allocate a fixed percentage of net profits to a trading R&D account.
- Sequence:
  1. backtesting
  2. paper trading
  3. small-size live deployment
  4. scale only after risk-adjusted benchmarks are met
- Start with Forex only if brokerage/legal constraints are satisfied in jurisdiction.

### 3.5 Treasury Management
- Maintain basic ledger: revenue, costs, taxes, reserves, deployable capital.
- Move owner payouts to Cash App **$Nicsins** only after reserve and tax buffers are funded.
- Enforce weekly reconciliation checkpoints.

### 3.6 Content Engine
- Document every sprint:
  - what was built
  - what worked
  - what failed
  - next hypothesis
- Convert process docs into:
  - tutorial/course modules
  - YouTube episode outlines
  - narrative arc featuring an anthropomorphic protagonist pursuing a mech suit + robot body goal

---

## 4. 90-Day Execution Plan

### Days 1-30: Foundations
- Finalize guardrails and compliance checklist.
- Stand up compliant data ingestion + RAG baseline.
- Launch first monetization micro-offers and tracking dashboard.

### Days 31-60: Revenue Engine
- Double down on channels with strongest profit per hour.
- Add automation for prospect qualification and campaign generation.
- Publish first public tutorial/content pieces.

### Days 61-90: Scale + Trading Readiness
- Formalize SOPs and delegation triggers for sub-agents.
- Reach consistent monthly profit target.
- Begin simulator/paper-trading stage and define go-live criteria.

---

## 5. Hourly Agent Loop (for Cron Automations)
1. Review latest KPIs and blockers.
2. Pick highest expected-value legal task.
3. Execute one shippable change.
4. Update mission journal with metrics and next action.
5. Trigger follow-up subtask for the next cycle.

---

## 6. Success Metrics
- Positive monthly net cash flow for 3 consecutive months.
- At least 2 active compliant revenue channels.
- Contact database with consent provenance for every record.
- Weekly content output cadence maintained.
- Trading remains in simulation until risk gates are objectively passed.
