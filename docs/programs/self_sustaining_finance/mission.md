# Program Blueprint: Self-Sustaining Financial System

## Mission

Build a self-sustaining online income engine that compounds into disciplined, risk-managed investing while maintaining legal and ethical compliance.

## Important Scope Correction

This program explicitly does **not** include selling scraped or non-consensual email lists.  
All contact acquisition and outreach must be consent-based and auditable under `docs/policies/compliance_pack.md`.

## Objectives (12-Month Horizon)

1. Launch at least two recurring online revenue streams.
2. Build a consented audience funnel (newsletter + CRM) with documented opt-ins.
3. Reach positive monthly cash flow before activating live trading.
4. Deploy a rules-based trading workflow only after paper-trading validation.
5. Publish a full public build log plus educational content series.

## Phase Plan

### Phase 0 - Foundation (Weeks 1-2)
- Stand up mission governance:
  - compliance checks
  - KPI dashboard
  - weekly journal cadence
- Define offer stack:
  - one service offer
  - one digital product concept
  - one affiliate niche
- Set system of record:
  - CRM for consented contacts
  - accounting ledger
  - content tracker

### Phase 1 - Monetization Engine (Weeks 3-12)
- Lead generation (consent-first):
  - opt-in landing pages
  - newsletter signup magnets
  - social and content funnels
- Revenue channels:
  - productized services
  - digital downloads/templates
  - affiliate partnerships with disclosure
- Automation:
  - qualification scoring
  - follow-up sequences with unsubscribe controls
  - weekly KPI exports

### Phase 2 - Capital Discipline and Trading Prep (Weeks 13-20)
- Allocate a fixed percentage of net monthly profit to reserve capital.
- Build and validate trading system:
  - paper trading only
  - strategy backtests
  - risk limits and stop logic
- Go-live gate:
  - minimum paper-trading consistency target
  - max drawdown threshold
  - documented runbook approved by Compliance Guardian

### Phase 3 - Scale and Media Flywheel (Weeks 21-52)
- Reinvest into:
  - better acquisition channels
  - higher-value products/services
  - analytics tooling
- Produce educational assets:
  - process documentation
  - course outline
  - YouTube episodes
- Story layer:
  - anthropomorphic narrator persona
  - serialized narrative about funding a mech suit and robot body

## Workstreams

1. **Agentic Framework**
   - Build modular agents for research, content, CRM ops, finance ops, and compliance checks.
2. **Data/RAG Pipeline**
   - Use RAG on authorized documents and communications only.
   - Extract contacts only when lawful basis and consent are present.
3. **Monetization**
   - Prioritize recurring revenue and repeatable fulfillment.
4. **Finance Operations**
   - Weekly reconciliation, reserves policy, transfer logs.
5. **Education and Brand**
   - Tutorial/course production and narrative packaging.

## KPIs

- Monthly revenue, gross margin, and free cash flow.
- Cost per qualified lead and lead-to-sale conversion.
- Newsletter growth and unsubscribe/spam complaint rate.
- Paper-trading expectancy, win rate, and drawdown.
- Content output cadence and watch-time retention.

## Guardrails

- No unauthorized account access.
- No personal-data brokerage.
- No deceptive claims or impersonation.
- No live trading until paper-trading criteria are met.
