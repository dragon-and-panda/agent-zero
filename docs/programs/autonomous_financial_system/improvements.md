# Autonomous Financial System — Improvement Backlog

Ranked by expected business impact and risk reduction.

1. **Consent Ledger Schema**
   - Add standardized fields: source, consent status, timestamp, retention policy, and revocation path.
   - Success metric: 100% of active contacts have provenance + consent metadata.

2. **Experiment Scorecard Automation**
   - Track offer/channel experiments with weekly rollups (revenue, CAC, conversion, margin).
   - Success metric: every experiment has a pass/fail decision within 7 days.

3. **Risk Dashboard v1**
   - Implement capital buckets, drawdown monitor, and payout readiness checks.
   - Success metric: no payout issued without reconciliation + reserve checks.
