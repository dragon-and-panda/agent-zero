# Compliant Growth Policy Pack

This policy pack is mandatory for all growth, outreach, data, and monetization workflows.

## 1) Data and consent
- Collect and process only first-party or explicitly consented data.
- Maintain consent timestamp, source, and allowed purpose for every contact.
- Honor unsubscribe and suppression requests immediately.
- Reject datasets with unknown provenance.

## 2) Outreach and messaging
- No purchased, scraped, or harvested personal email lists.
- No deceptive sender identity, headers, or subject lines.
- Every campaign must include a clear unsubscribe mechanism.
- Apply regional legal requirements before sending (e.g., CAN-SPAM/GDPR equivalents where applicable).

## 3) Platform and ToS adherence
- Follow each platform's Terms of Service and acceptable-use policy.
- If a platform disallows a workflow, the workflow is blocked by default.
- Any ambiguity routes to Compliance Guardian for review.

## 4) Financial controls
- Record every revenue event in the ledger with source reference.
- Reconcile before payouts or transfers.
- Maintain an operating reserve before capital allocation to speculative activities.

## 5) Trading controls
- Live trading prohibited until strategy passes documented validation gates.
- Enforce max risk-per-trade and drawdown stops.
- Trigger kill-switch on breach; require explicit re-approval after incident review.

## 6) Logging and auditability
- Log all material agent actions, tool calls, and policy decisions.
- Keep weekly compliance summaries in the mission diary.
- Retain records needed for disputes, chargebacks, and regulatory inquiries.

## 7) Escalation
- Any policy violation pauses affected automations.
- Compliance Guardian files incident summary with remediation steps.
- Re-enable only after controls are patched and verified.
