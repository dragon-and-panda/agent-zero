# Agentic Financial System Policy Pack

This policy pack governs data handling, outreach, monetization, and treasury actions for the agentic financial system.

## 1) Scope

Applies to all workflows, agents, scripts, and operators that process contact data, generate outreach, manage cashflow, or produce trading signals.

## 2) Core Principles

- **Consent first:** No promotional outreach to contacts without explicit opt-in status.
- **Data minimization:** Collect only what is required to deliver defined outcomes.
- **Purpose limitation:** Data use must match the documented purpose for which data was collected.
- **Auditability:** Every meaningful action must produce a verifiable log trail.
- **Human override:** High-risk actions require manual checkpoint approval.

## 3) Prohibited Activities

The system must not:
- sell or transfer personal contact lists,
- ingest private data without account-owner authorization,
- bypass platform terms or anti-spam requirements,
- impersonate identities or conceal sender intent,
- execute live trading without risk controls and validation gates.

## 4) Data Classification

- **Public:** Publicly available non-sensitive information.
- **Internal:** Operational data generated by this system.
- **Restricted:** Contact records, communication metadata, payout identifiers.

Restricted data must be encrypted at rest and in transit where feasible, and access must be role-limited.

## 5) Consent Model

Allowed `contact_status` values:
- `opt_in`
- `transactional_only`
- `no_consent`
- `do_not_contact`

### Outreach rule
- Promotional outreach is allowed only for `opt_in`.
- Transactional messages are allowed for `opt_in` and `transactional_only` when operationally necessary.
- `no_consent` and `do_not_contact` must be excluded from campaign targets.

## 6) Retention & Deletion

- Keep raw ingestion artifacts only as long as necessary for processing and audit.
- Delete or anonymize stale restricted data on a fixed cadence.
- Honor suppression and deletion requests with highest priority.
- Maintain immutable audit metadata even when payload data is deleted.

## 7) Monetization Compliance

Approved monetization categories:
- consent-based newsletters and sponsorships,
- automation setup services,
- internal tooling/templates/playbooks,
- educational content and courses.

Disallowed category:
- brokerage or sale of personal contact data.

## 8) Trading Risk Controls

Before live capital deployment:
1. Complete paper trading period.
2. Meet predefined strategy pass criteria.
3. Enforce per-trade risk and daily drawdown limits.
4. Log every trade rationale, execution, and post-analysis.

## 9) Treasury Controls

Each transfer to Cash App (`$Nicsins`) must include:
- source of funds,
- ledger reconciliation checkpoint,
- tax reserve checkpoint,
- transfer confirmation ID.

## 10) Incident Response

Severity levels:
- **SEV-1:** Data exposure, prohibited outreach at scale, unauthorized transfer.
- **SEV-2:** Policy violation with contained impact.
- **SEV-3:** Non-critical control failure.

Response workflow:
1. Pause affected automation.
2. Preserve logs and evidence.
3. Identify blast radius and remediate.
4. Record root cause and control updates.

## 11) Enforcement

- Any workflow that cannot prove consent eligibility must fail closed.
- Scripts and agents should run policy checks before external actions.
- Violations require remediation tasks in the improvement backlog before reactivation.
