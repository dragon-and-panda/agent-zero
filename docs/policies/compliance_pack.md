# Compliance Pack: Ethical Revenue + Data Governance

This policy is the mandatory guardrail for any autonomous workflow tied to financial growth, outreach, data processing, or content publishing.

## 1) Purpose

Create a durable online income system while protecting privacy, respecting law, and reducing operational/legal risk.

## 2) Non-Negotiable Rules

1. **No illegal data brokering.**  
   Do not collect, package, buy, sell, rent, or trade personal email lists sourced from private communications or unauthorized scraping.
2. **Consent-first outreach only.**  
   Email campaigns must target recipients who have opted in or have a lawful basis for contact.
3. **No credential abuse.**  
   Never exfiltrate credentials, bypass authentication, or access accounts/files without explicit authorization.
4. **No deceptive practices.**  
   No impersonation, fake sender identity, hidden affiliations, or misleading claims.
5. **Data minimization.**  
   Collect only the minimum data needed for the stated business objective.

## 3) Data Handling Standards

### Allowed
- Process the operator's own mailbox data for personal analytics, CRM hygiene, and relationship management.
- Build contact records only from:
  - Explicit opt-ins
  - Public business directories that permit commercial use
  - Direct inbound inquiries
- Store provenance for each contact (source, date, consent status).

### Prohibited
- Extracting and selling addresses from received/sent/CC email histories.
- Compiling contact lists from third-party files without verified rights.
- Any workflow designed primarily for spam or unsolicited bulk outreach.

## 4) Email + Outreach Compliance Checklist

Before any campaign is launched:
- Consent status is known and recorded.
- Unsubscribe mechanism is active and tested.
- Sender identity and business purpose are clear.
- Regional compliance checks are completed (for example: CAN-SPAM/GDPR/other applicable rules).
- Suppression list is applied.

## 5) Approved Monetization Tracks (Phase 1)

- Productized services (automation setup, analytics dashboards, content ops).
- Affiliate and partner programs with transparent disclosure.
- Paid educational products (course, templates, playbooks).
- Permission-based newsletter sponsorships.
- B2B lead qualification from lawful, documented sources.

## 6) Financial Expansion Controls (Trading)

- Start with paper trading and backtesting first.
- Use strict risk limits (for example: fixed max daily drawdown and per-trade risk caps).
- Keep capital segregation between business operations and trading accounts.
- Maintain an execution journal and strategy review cadence.

## 7) Cash Management SOP

- Maintain a weekly payout cadence from operating accounts to the designated cash account.
- Log each transfer with timestamp, source, amount, and purpose.
- Reconcile transfers weekly against revenue and expense ledgers.

## 8) Enforcement

Any agent or workflow violating this pack must:
1. Stop immediately.
2. Log an incident.
3. Escalate to the Risk & Ethics Governor (or human operator).
4. Remain paused until policy-compliant remediation is approved.
