# Compliance Pack — Agentic Financial System

This policy pack defines mandatory constraints for any autonomous workflow tied to revenue generation, outreach, and finance operations.

## 1) Non-Negotiable Guardrails
- Do not scrape, export, sell, or share personal email addresses without explicit, informed consent.
- Do not perform unauthorized access to Gmail, Google Workspace, or any third-party account.
- Do not run spam campaigns, deceptive outreach, or evasion tactics.
- Do not provide regulated financial advice without proper licensing and disclosure.
- Do not execute live trading until paper-trading and risk controls pass acceptance thresholds.

## 2) Approved Data Collection Patterns
- Opt-in forms with clear consent language (newsletter, waitlist, lead magnet).
- First-party CRM/contact lists where users can unsubscribe at any time.
- Public business contacts only when outreach is lawful, relevant, and policy-compliant.
- Internal operational logs that exclude sensitive personal content unless strictly necessary.

## 3) Email and RAG Data Rules
- Use retrieval on email data only for owner-approved analytics and workflow automation.
- Minimize data scope: collect only fields needed for the objective.
- Hash or redact personal identifiers when full identity is not required.
- Maintain deletion and retention controls for all ingested records.

## 4) Monetization Constraints
- Allowed: consulting, digital products, software subscriptions, affiliate partnerships, lawful lead generation.
- Disallowed: selling harvested contact lists, data brokerage without consent, or platform policy circumvention.
- Each monetization channel must include Terms-of-Service review and compliance check before launch.

## 5) Trading and Treasury Controls
- Start with paper trading and strategy validation; graduate to live capital in small, pre-set tranches.
- Enforce position sizing and drawdown caps (example: 0.5-1.0% risk per trade, 5% max weekly drawdown).
- Keep a verifiable ledger of cashflow, fees, and transfer events.
- Any transfer destination (including Cash App handles) must be owner-verified before execution.

## 6) Audit Requirements
- Every automation run should log:
  - objective,
  - tools used,
  - data sources touched,
  - compliance checks passed/failed,
  - financial impact.
- High-risk actions (payments, data exports, live trades) require an explicit checkpoint entry in the mission journal.

## 7) Escalation Triggers
- Immediate halt on suspected policy breach, credential misuse, or privacy incident.
- Immediate halt if trading drawdown limits are exceeded.
- Immediate halt when platform terms conflict with planned automation.
