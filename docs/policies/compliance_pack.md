# Compliance Pack for Monetization and Contact Data

This policy pack governs any revenue workflow that touches communications data, lead generation, outreach, payments, or capital allocation.

---

## 1. Core Rule

The system may only pursue revenue through lawful, ethical, consent-based methods.

If a tactic depends on unauthorized access, privacy invasion, spam, deception, or sale of personal data, it is out of scope.

---

## 2. Contact and Inbox Data Rules

### Allowed
- Process mailbox data only for the mailbox owner's direct benefit and with their authorization.
- Extract contacts for the owner's CRM, relationship mapping, follow-up support, or analytics.
- Summarize threads, deduplicate contacts, and categorize communications for internal use by the owner.

### Prohibited
- Selling, renting, or brokering contact lists extracted from emails.
- Harvesting addresses from received, sent, or CC data for third-party marketing.
- Using inbox-derived contacts for unsolicited bulk outreach.
- Expanding into "lookalike" acquisition from non-public personal data.

### Data Handling Minimums
- Collect the minimum necessary data.
- Keep an audit trail of source, purpose, and retention decision.
- Support deletion/export on request.
- Separate customer workspaces and avoid cross-client reuse of raw contact data.

---

## 3. Lead Generation Rules

### Approved Methods
- inbound content,
- consent-based newsletter signups,
- public-web company research,
- referrals,
- direct outreach to businesses using public business contact channels when messaging is relevant, truthful, and low-volume.

### Disallowed Methods
- scraping private or authenticated systems without permission,
- sending bulk cold spam,
- using fake identities,
- bypassing rate limits or anti-abuse systems,
- buying or selling shady data sets with unclear provenance.

---

## 4. Payments and Fund Movement

- The repo may document bookkeeping and payout workflows, but it must not autonomously move funds without an approved and auditable integration.
- Any transfer destination must be configured explicitly outside the model's free-form reasoning.
- Manual review is required for payment routing changes.

---

## 5. Trading and Capital Allocation

Trading is not a valid first revenue source for this program.

Before any live deployment:

1. core operating revenue must already exist,
2. risk capital must be separated from operating cash,
3. the strategy must pass paper trading or replay testing,
4. drawdown limits and stop conditions must be documented,
5. leverage must default to off unless an external policy explicitly authorizes it.

Until then, trading ideas remain research-only.

---

## 6. Review Checklist

Every monetization idea must answer:

1. Is it legal in the target jurisdiction?
2. Is the data source consented, public, or licensed?
3. Would a reasonable customer understand and approve the workflow?
4. Could the tactic damage platform standing or brand trust?
5. Is there a safer alternative that preserves most of the value?

If any answer fails, reject or redesign the idea before execution.
