# Financial System Compliance Checklist

Use this checklist before running any acquisition, outreach, monetization, or capital allocation workflow.

## A. Data Collection and Privacy
- [ ] Data source is first-party, licensed, or explicitly permitted for use.
- [ ] Contact records include consent status and acquisition provenance.
- [ ] Collection and processing comply with applicable privacy laws (for example GDPR/CCPA/CAN-SPAM where relevant).
- [ ] No unauthorized mailbox scraping or private contact extraction.
- [ ] No sale or transfer of personal email lists without explicit legal basis and consent.

## B. Outreach and Distribution
- [ ] Outreach target and message comply with platform Terms of Service.
- [ ] Every campaign includes opt-out/unsubscribe mechanisms where required.
- [ ] Messaging content avoids deception, impersonation, and misleading claims.
- [ ] Domain reputation protections are enabled (rate limits, warm-up, bounce handling).

## C. Monetization and Offer Integrity
- [ ] Offer value proposition is truthful and verifiable.
- [ ] Pricing and refund policies are documented and accessible.
- [ ] Service delivery SOP is defined and measurable.
- [ ] Revenue events are logged for audit and reconciliation.

## D. Financial Operations
- [ ] Revenue split rules are configured (operations/reserve/growth/investment).
- [ ] Weekly reconciliation task is scheduled.
- [ ] Cash transfers (including Cash App) are recorded with timestamps and references.
- [ ] Tax and reporting obligations are tracked for all active jurisdictions.

## E. Trading and Capital Deployment
- [ ] Strategy is paper-tested before live deployment.
- [ ] Max daily loss and max drawdown limits are configured.
- [ ] Position sizing and stop-loss rules are automated.
- [ ] Kill-switch workflow is tested and available.
- [ ] Live capital is deployed only after pre-defined validation thresholds are met.

## F. Content and Media
- [ ] Published tutorials disclose material risks and avoid guaranteed-income claims.
- [ ] Storytelling elements are clearly marked as narrative when applicable.
- [ ] Claims about results are tied to logged metrics.

## G. Go/No-Go Decision
- [ ] All required items are checked.
- [ ] Risk and Ethics Governor approval logged.
- [ ] If any item fails, workflow is blocked until corrected.
