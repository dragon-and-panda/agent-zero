# Compliant Data and Growth Policy

This policy applies to all autonomous workflows that process contact data, run outreach, or monetize audience assets.

## 1) Prohibited Actions

- Selling or sharing private contact lists collected from inbox scraping.
- Using personal data without legal basis or documented consent.
- Sending marketing communications without opt-out capability.
- Misrepresenting trading returns as guaranteed outcomes.

## 2) Required Controls

- Maintain source provenance for all contacts.
- Track consent status on every record.
- Enforce suppression lists (`do_not_contact`, bounced, unsubscribed).
- Log all outbound campaign batches and reconciliation events.

## 3) Data Retention and Deletion

- Retain only data needed for active business workflows.
- Honor deletion and unsubscribe requests promptly.
- Purge stale or invalid records on a fixed cadence.

## 4) Enforcement

Any workflow violating this policy must be automatically paused and escalated to the Risk & Ethics Governor for review before resuming.
